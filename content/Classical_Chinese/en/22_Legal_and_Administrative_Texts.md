## 1. Administrative Writing: Overview

Administrative and legal writing in Classical Chinese follows strict conventions of format, vocabulary, and rhetorical structure. These texts governed the actual operations of the state across East Asia for over two millennia.

### Types of Administrative Documents

| Type | Hanja | Korean | Direction | Purpose |
|------|-------|--------|-----------|---------|
| Memorial | 上疏 | 상소 (sangso) | Official → King | Advice, petition, protest |
| Edict | 詔勅 | 조칙 (jochik) | King → Officials | Royal command |
| Proclamation | 檄文 | 격문 (gyeokmun) | General/Leader → Public | Rally, denounce |
| Report | 啓 | 계 (gye) | Official → Superior | Inform of situation |
| Letter | 書 | 서 (seo) | Peer → Peer | Diplomatic correspondence |
| Decree | 令 | 령 (ryeong) | Authority → Subordinate | Specific order |
| Appointment | 敎旨 | 교지 (gyoji) | King → Official | Grant of position |
| Judgment | 判 | 판 (pan) | Judge → Case | Legal ruling |

### Characteristics of Administrative Style

| Feature | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| Formality | Extreme deference, self-deprecation |
| Precision | Exact terminology, no ambiguity |
| Structure | Rigid format with prescribed sections |
| Tone | Solemn, measured, authoritative |
| Vocabulary | Specialized bureaucratic terms |
| Length | Can be very long (hundreds of characters) |

## 2. Sangso: Memorial to the Throne

### 상소문/上疏文 — Complete Structure

A 상소문 follows a prescribed sequence of sections:

| Section | Opening Formula | Korean | Function |
|---------|----------------|--------|----------|
| 1. Prostration | 伏以 | 복이 | Prostrating, I submit... |
| 2. Self-deprecation | 臣不佞 | 신불녕 | Your unworthy servant... |
| 3. Background | 竊惟 | 절유 | I humbly consider... |
| 4. Main argument | 臣以爲 | 신이위 | Your servant believes... |
| 5. Evidence | 古者...今者... | 고자...금자... | In ancient times... now... |
| 6. Request | 伏望殿下 | 복망전하 | I humbly hope Your Majesty... |
| 7. Closing | 臣不勝懇切屏營之至 | 신불승간절병영지지 | Your servant cannot contain... |

### Model 상소문: 이이(李珥) — 만언봉사/萬言封事

The "Ten Thousand Character Memorial" by 이이(李珥, Yulgok) is one of the most famous 상소 in Korean history.

**Opening**:

```
臣伏惟 殿下以聰明之資
신복유 전하이총명지자

嗣大統 臨御以來
사대통 임어이래

勵精求治 日新又新
려정구치 일신우신
```

### Detailed Analysis

| Phrase | Reading | Translation |
|--------|---------|-------------|
| 臣伏惟 | 신복유 | Your prostrating servant humbly considers |
| 殿下 | 전하 | Your Majesty |
| 以聰明之資 | 이총명지자 | with the endowment of brilliance |
| 嗣大統 | 사대통 | succeeded to the great succession (throne) |
| 臨御以來 | 임어이래 | since assuming governance |
| 勵精求治 | 려정구치 | exerting vigor to seek good governance |
| 日新又新 | 일신우신 | renewing daily and again renewing |

Key vocabulary:

| Character | Reading | Meaning |
|-----------|---------|---------|
| 臣 | 신 (sin) | your servant (first person to king) |
| 伏 | 복 (bok) | to prostrate |
| 惟 | 유 (yu) | to consider, think |
| 聰明 | 총명 (chongmyeong) | brilliant, sagacious |
| 資 | 자 (ja) | endowment, material |
| 嗣 | 사 (sa) | to succeed, inherit |
| 統 | 통 (tong) | succession, lineage |
| 勵 | 려 (ryeo) | to exert, encourage |
| 精 | 정 (jeong) | vigor, essence |

## 3. Jochik: Imperial Edicts

### 조칙/詔勅 — Royal Command Format

Edicts issued by the king followed their own rigid format:

| Section | Formula | Function |
|---------|---------|----------|
| Opening | 王若曰 (왕약왈) | "The King speaks thus:" |
| Address | 咨爾群臣 (자이군신) | "To you, ministers and officials" |
| Reason | 朕惟 (짐유) | "We consider that..." |
| Command | 宜...可... | "It is appropriate to... shall..." |
| Warning | 其勿違 (기물위) | "Do not disobey" |
| Closing | 故玆詔示 (고자조시) | "Therefore this edict is promulgated" |

### Key Edict Vocabulary

| Term | Hanja | Reading | Meaning |
|------|-------|---------|---------|
| We/I (royal) | 朕 | 짐 (jim) | Royal first person |
| Speak thus | 若曰 | 약왈 (yakwal) | "speaks as follows" |
| To you | 咨爾 | 자이 (jai) | Addressing officials |
| It is fitting | 宜 | 의 (ui) | It is appropriate |
| Shall | 可 | 가 (ga) | Should, may |
| Promulgate | 詔示 | 조시 (josi) | To announce by edict |
| Do not violate | 勿違 | 물위 (mulwi) | Must not disobey |
| In perpetuity | 永爲 | 영위 (yeongwi) | Permanently establish |

### Sample Edict

```
王若曰
왕약왈

朕惟治國之道 在於得人
짐유치국지도 재어득인

今有某 才德兼備
금유모 재덕겸비

可授某官 其勿辭
가수모관 기물사

故玆詔示
고자조시
```

| Line | Translation |
|------|-------------|
| 王若曰 | The King speaks thus: |
| 朕惟治國之道 在於得人 | We consider that the way of governing the nation lies in finding the right people |
| 今有某 才德兼備 | Now there is So-and-so, who possesses both talent and virtue |
| 可授某官 其勿辭 | He shall be granted such-and-such position; he must not decline |
| 故玆詔示 | Therefore this edict is hereby promulgated |

## 4. Gyeokmun: Proclamations

### 격문/檄文 — Military and Public Proclamations

A 격문 is a public document meant to rally support, denounce enemies, or announce a cause.

### Structure

| Section | Function | Rhetorical Goal |
|---------|----------|----------------|
| Opening | State the injustice | Anger |
| Evidence | List crimes of the enemy | Outrage |
| Appeal to virtue | Invoke righteousness | Moral clarity |
| Call to action | Command response | Motivation |
| Warning | Threaten the disobedient | Urgency |

### 이순신(李舜臣): 격문 Excerpt

```
嗟爾倭賊 犯我疆土
차이왜적 범아강토

殺我百姓 焚我宗廟
살아백성 분아종묘

天地鬼神之所共怒也
천지귀신지소공노야
```

### Analysis

| Phrase | Reading | Translation |
|--------|---------|-------------|
| 嗟爾倭賊 | 차이왜적 | You wretched Japanese invaders! |
| 犯我疆土 | 범아강토 | You have violated our territory |
| 殺我百姓 | 살아백성 | You have killed our people |
| 焚我宗廟 | 분아종묘 | You have burned our ancestral shrines |
| 天地鬼神之所共怒也 | 천지귀신지소공노야 | Heaven, earth, and the spirits are all enraged |

Key vocabulary:

| Character | Reading | Meaning |
|-----------|---------|---------|
| 嗟 | 차 (cha) | alas! (exclamation of contempt) |
| 倭 | 왜 (wae) | Japanese (historical, often pejorative) |
| 賊 | 적 (jeok) | bandit, enemy |
| 犯 | 범 (beom) | to violate, invade |
| 疆土 | 강토 (gangto) | territory |
| 焚 | 분 (bun) | to burn |
| 宗廟 | 종묘 (jongmyo) | ancestral shrine |
| 怒 | 노 (no) | anger, rage |

### Rhetorical Analysis

| Device | Instance | Effect |
|--------|----------|--------|
| Parallelism | 犯我疆土 / 殺我百姓 / 焚我宗廟 | Rhythmic escalation |
| Repetition | 我 (our) repeated | Collective identity |
| Hyperbole | 天地鬼神之所共怒 | Cosmic indignation |
| Direct address | 嗟爾倭賊 | Confrontational tone |

## 5. Legal Terminology

### The Four Categories of Law (四法/사법)

| Category | Hanja | Reading | Function |
|----------|-------|---------|----------|
| Statutes | 律 | 률 (ryul) | Criminal law, penalties |
| Ordinances | 令 | 령 (ryeong) | Administrative regulations |
| Formats | 格 | 격 (gyeok) | Procedural forms |
| Standards | 式 | 식 (sik) | Standard templates |

### Criminal Law Terms

| Term | Hanja | Reading | Meaning |
|------|-------|---------|---------|
| Crime | 罪 | 죄 (joe) | Offense, crime |
| Punishment | 刑 | 형 (hyeong) | Penalty, punishment |
| Death penalty | 死刑 | 사형 (sahyeong) | Capital punishment |
| Exile | 流 / 流刑 | 유/유형 (yu/yuhyeong) | Banishment |
| Flogging | 笞 | 태 (tae) | Beating with thin rod |
| Caning | 杖 | 장 (jang) | Beating with thick rod |
| Imprisonment | 徒 | 도 (do) | Penal servitude |
| Pardon | 赦 | 사 (sa) | Amnesty |
| Accusation | 訴 | 소 (so) | Lawsuit, complaint |
| Judge | 判官 | 판관 (pan-gwan) | Adjudicating official |
| Verdict | 判決 | 판결 (pangyeol) | Judicial decision |
| Evidence | 證 | 증 (jeung) | Proof, testimony |

### The Five Punishments (五刑/오형)

Traditional East Asian law recognized five grades of punishment:

| Grade | Hanja | Reading | Description |
|-------|-------|---------|-------------|
| 1st (lightest) | 笞 | 태 (tae) | Light beating (10-50 strokes) |
| 2nd | 杖 | 장 (jang) | Heavy beating (60-100 strokes) |
| 3rd | 徒 | 도 (do) | Forced labor (1-3 years) |
| 4th | 流 | 유 (yu) | Exile (2000-3000 li) |
| 5th (heaviest) | 死 | 사 (sa) | Death |

## 7. The Legal Code System

### Major Korean Legal Codes

| Code | Hanja | Reading | Period | Purpose |
|------|-------|---------|--------|---------|
| 경국대전 | 經國大典 | gyeongguk daejeon | 1469 | Comprehensive code of Joseon |
| 속대전 | 續大典 | sok daejeon | 1746 | Supplement to 경국대전 |
| 대전통편 | 大典通編 | daejeon tongpyeon | 1785 | Consolidated code |
| 대전회통 | 大典會通 | daejeon hoetong | 1865 | Final comprehensive code |

### Structure of 경국대전/經國大典

The code was organized by the six ministries:

| Section | Ministry | Topics |
|---------|----------|--------|
| 吏典 (이전) | Personnel | Officials, appointments, ranks |
| 戶典 (호전) | Revenue | Taxation, land, population |
| 禮典 (예전) | Rites | Ceremonies, education, exams |
| 兵典 (병전) | Military | Army, defense, posts |
| 刑典 (형전) | Justice | Laws, punishments, trials |
| 工典 (공전) | Works | Construction, crafts, slaves |

### Sample Legal Provision

```
凡官吏受賕者 計贓科斷
범관리수뢰자 계장과단
```

| Phrase | Reading | Translation |
|--------|---------|-------------|
| 凡 | 범 (beom) | In all cases |
| 官吏 | 관리 (gwalli) | Officials and clerks |
| 受賕者 | 수뢰자 (suroe-ja) | Those who receive bribes |
| 計贓 | 계장 (gyejang) | Calculate the amount of stolen goods |
| 科斷 | 과단 (gwadan) | Determine the penalty |

## 8. Sample Texts with Annotation

### Full Annotated 상소문 Excerpt

From a Joseon-period memorial protesting a policy:

```
臣聞 治世之道 在得其人
신문 치세지도 재득기인

得其人則治 失其人則亂
득기인즉치 실기인즉란

此古今之通論也
차고금지통론야

今殿下所任用者 非其人也
금전하소임용자 비기인야

臣不勝憂慮之至
신불승우려지지
```

### Line-by-Line Analysis

| Line | Reading | Translation |
|------|---------|-------------|
| 臣聞 | 신문 | Your servant has heard |
| 治世之道 在得其人 | 치세지도 재득기인 | The way to govern the world lies in finding the right people |
| 得其人則治 | 득기인즉치 | If you find the right people, there is order |
| 失其人則亂 | 실기인즉란 | If you lose them, there is chaos |
| 此古今之通論也 | 차고금지통론야 | This is a universal principle of ancient and modern times |
| 今殿下所任用者 | 금전하소임용자 | Those whom Your Majesty currently employs |
| 非其人也 | 비기인야 | Are not the right people |
| 臣不勝憂慮之至 | 신불승우려지지 | Your servant cannot contain his worry |

### Grammatical Notes

| Element | Function |
|---------|----------|
| 臣聞 | Formulaic: "Your servant has heard" — introduces a general principle |
| 則 | Conditional marker: "if... then..." |
| 此...也 | Emphatic judgment: "This is..." |
| 今 | Temporal transition: "Now" / "Currently" |
| 所任用者 | 所+V+者: "those who are employed" |
| 非...也 | Negative judgment: "are not..." |
| 不勝...之至 | Formulaic: "cannot contain the extreme of..." |
