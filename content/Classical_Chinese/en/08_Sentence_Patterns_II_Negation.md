## 1. Overview of Negation in Classical Chinese

### 1.1 Six Ways to Say "No"

Classical Chinese has multiple negation words, each with a distinct nuance. Choosing the right one is essential for precise expression.

> **Six Negation Words at a Glance**
>
> - **不 (불/부)**: "not" (general negation of verbs/adjectives)
> - **非 (비)**: "is not" (negation of identity/equation)
> - **無 (무)**: "there is no" (negation of existence)
> - **未 (미)**: "not yet" (temporal: hasn't happened yet)
> - **莫 (막)**: "no one / nothing" (universal negation)
> - **勿 (물)**: "do not" (prohibition/imperative)
>
> Korean equivalents:
> - **不** = -지 않다 / 안-, **非** = -이 아니다
> - **無** = 없다, **未** = 아직 -지 않다
> - **莫** = 아무도 -지 않다, **勿** = -하지 마라

### 1.2 Quick Comparison

| Negation | Type | What it Negates | Example | Translation |
|----------|------|----------------|---------|-------------|
| 不 | General | Verbs, adjectives | 不知 | Does not know |
| 非 | Identity | "A is B" equations | 非人 | Is not a person |
| 無 | Existence | "There is X" | 無道 | There is no Way |
| 未 | Temporal | Actions not yet done | 未學 | Has not yet studied |
| 莫 | Universal | No one, nothing | 莫知 | No one knows |
| 勿 | Prohibition | Imperative negative | 勿言 | Do not speak |

## 2. 不 — General Negation

### 2.1 Basic Usage

不 (불/부, bul/bu) is the most common negation word. It negates verbs and adjectives.

| Character | 음독 | 훈독 | Function |
|-----------|------|------|----------|
| 不 | 불/부 (bul/bu) | 아닐 불 | General negation of verbs and adjectives |

> **Pronunciation note**: 不 is normally read 불 (bul), but becomes 부 (bu) before certain syllables, e.g., 不當 → 부당.

### 2.2 不 + Verb

| Sentence | Reading | Translation |
|----------|---------|-------------|
| 不知 | 부지 | Does not know |
| 不學 | 불학 | Does not study |
| 不行 | 불행 | Does not go; does not practice |
| 不言 | 불언 | Does not speak |
| 不食 | 불식 | Does not eat |
| 不見 | 불견 | Does not see |

### 2.3 不 + Adjective

| Sentence | Reading | Translation |
|----------|---------|-------------|
| 不善 | 불선 | Is not good |
| 不義 | 불의 | Is not righteous |
| 不仁 | 불인 | Is not benevolent |
| 不足 | 부족 | Is not sufficient; lacking |
| 不遠 | 불원 | Is not far |

### 2.4 不 in Complete Sentences

| Sentence | Reading | Translation |
|----------|---------|-------------|
| 人不學 | 인불학 | A person does not study |
| 王不仁 | 왕불인 | The king is not benevolent |
| 天不言 | 천불언 | Heaven does not speak |
| 子不食肉 | 자불식육 | The child does not eat meat |
| 人不知而不慍 | 인부지이불온 | Others do not know [me], yet I am not resentful |

### 2.5 Common 不 Compounds in Korean

| Compound | Reading | Meaning |
|----------|---------|---------|
| 不可 | 불가 | Impossible; unacceptable |
| 不足 | 부족 | Insufficient; lacking |
| 不當 | 부당 | Unfair; improper |
| 不正 | 부정 | Dishonest; unjust |
| 不幸 | 불행 | Unhappy; unfortunate |
| 不安 | 불안 | Uneasy; anxious |
| 不義 | 불의 | Unrighteous; unjust |
| 不信 | 불신 | Distrust |

## 3. 非 — Negation of Identity

### 3.1 Basic Usage

非 (비, bi) negates identity — it says "X is NOT Y." It negates the copula (是/也), not a verb or adjective.

| Character | 음독 | 훈독 | Function |
|-----------|------|------|----------|
| 非 | 비 (bi) | 아닐 비 | Negation of identity: "is not" |

### 3.2 非 vs 不

| Negation | What It Negates | Example | Translation |
|----------|----------------|---------|-------------|
| 不 | Verb/Adjective | 不善 | Is not good (quality) |
| 非 | Identity/Equation | 非善也 | [This] is not goodness (identity) |

### 3.3 非 Examples

| Sentence | Reading | Translation |
|----------|---------|-------------|
| 非人也 | 비인야 | [This] is not a person / not humane |
| 非禮也 | 비례야 | [This] is not proper ritual |
| 非吾所知也 | 비오소지야 | [This] is not what I know |
| 非學者之道也 | 비학자지도야 | [This] is not the way of a scholar |
| 此非義也 | 차비의야 | This is not righteousness |

### 3.4 非...也 Pattern

非 often pairs with 也 in a full negation-of-identity sentence:

| Sentence | Reading | Translation |
|----------|---------|-------------|
| 此非禮也 | 차비례야 | This is not [proper] ritual |
| 彼非君子也 | 피비군자야 | He is not a gentleman |
| 戰非善事也 | 전비선사야 | War is not a good thing |

### 3.5 非 in Korean Compounds

| Compound | Reading | Meaning |
|----------|---------|---------|
| 非常 | 비상 | Extraordinary; emergency |
| 是非 | 시비 | Right and wrong; dispute |
| 非難 | 비난 | Blame; criticism |
| 非法 | 비법 | Illegal |

## 4. 無 — Negation of Existence

### 4.1 Basic Usage

無 (무, mu) negates existence — it says "there is no X" or "X does not exist." It is the negative counterpart of 有 (유, "there is").

| Character | 음독 | 훈독 | Function |
|-----------|------|------|----------|
| 無 | 무 (mu) | 없을 무 | Negation of existence: "there is no; without" |

### 4.2 有 vs 無

| Positive | Negative | Reading | Translation |
|----------|----------|---------|-------------|
| 有道 | 無道 | 유도 / 무도 | There is the Way / There is no Way |
| 有人 | 無人 | 유인 / 무인 | There are people / There are no people |
| 有心 | 無心 | 유심 / 무심 | Having heart/intention / Having no intention |
| 有禮 | 無禮 | 유례 / 무례 | Having propriety / Being rude |

### 4.3 無 in Complete Sentences

| Sentence | Reading | Translation |
|----------|---------|-------------|
| 天下無道 | 천하무도 | There is no Way in the world |
| 國無賢臣 | 국무현신 | The country has no worthy ministers |
| 人無信不立 | 인무신불립 | Without trust, a person cannot stand |
| 無父無君 | 무부무군 | Without father, without ruler (extreme disrespect) |
| 吾無隱乎爾 | 오무은호이 | I hide nothing from you |

### 4.4 無 in Korean Compounds

| Compound | Reading | Meaning |
|----------|---------|---------|
| 無限 | 무한 | Unlimited; infinite |
| 無理 | 무리 | Unreasonable; excessive |
| 無知 | 무지 | Ignorant |
| 無名 | 무명 | Nameless; unknown |
| 無禮 | 무례 | Rude; impolite |
| 無事 | 무사 | Uneventful; safe |
| 無心 | 무심 | Indifferent; unintentional |
| 無用 | 무용 | Useless |

## 5. 未 — Temporal Negation

### 5.1 Basic Usage

未 (미, mi) means "not yet" — the action has not happened but may happen in the future.

| Character | 음독 | 훈독 | Function |
|-----------|------|------|----------|
| 未 | 미 (mi) | 아직 미 | Temporal negation: "not yet" |

### 5.2 未 vs 不

| Negation | Meaning | Example | Translation |
|----------|---------|---------|-------------|
| 不知 | Does not know (general) | 人不知 | People do not know |
| 未知 | Does not yet know (temporal) | 人未知 | People do not yet know |

### 5.3 未 Examples

| Sentence | Reading | Translation |
|----------|---------|-------------|
| 未學 | 미학 | Has not yet studied |
| 未知 | 미지 | Does not yet know; unknown |
| 未見 | 미견 | Has not yet seen |
| 未聞 | 미문 | Has not yet heard; unprecedented |
| 吾未見仁者 | 오미견인자 | I have not yet seen a truly benevolent person |
| 道未行也 | 도미행야 | The Way has not yet been put into practice |

### 5.4 未 in Korean Compounds

| Compound | Reading | Meaning |
|----------|---------|---------|
| 未來 | 미래 | Future ("not yet come") |
| 未知 | 미지 | Unknown |
| 未定 | 미정 | Undecided |
| 未滿 | 미만 | Under; less than |
| 未成年 | 미성년 | Minor; underage |

## 6. 莫 — Universal Negation

### 6.1 Basic Usage

莫 (막, mak) means "no one" or "nothing" — it negates universally, covering all possible subjects or objects.

| Character | 음독 | 훈독 | Function |
|-----------|------|------|----------|
| 莫 | 막 (mak) | 없을 막 | Universal negation: "no one; nothing; cannot" |

### 6.2 莫 as "No One"

| Sentence | Reading | Translation |
|----------|---------|-------------|
| 莫知 | 막지 | No one knows |
| 莫敢言 | 막감언 | No one dares to speak |
| 莫能行 | 막능행 | No one can carry it out |
| 天下莫不知 | 천하막부지 | No one in the world does not know (= everyone knows) |

### 6.3 莫 as "Nothing"

| Sentence | Reading | Translation |
|----------|---------|-------------|
| 莫大於天 | 막대어천 | Nothing is greater than heaven |
| 莫善於仁 | 막선어인 | Nothing is better than benevolence |
| 莫重於義 | 막중어의 | Nothing is weightier than righteousness |

### 6.4 莫 as Prohibition (literary)

In some contexts, 莫 functions as a prohibition, similar to 勿:

| Sentence | Reading | Translation |
|----------|---------|-------------|
| 莫言 | 막언 | Do not speak / say nothing |
| 莫忘 | 막망 | Do not forget |

## 7. 勿 — Prohibition

### 7.1 Basic Usage

勿 (물, mul) is used for prohibitions — "do not!" commands.

| Character | 음독 | 훈독 | Function |
|-----------|------|------|----------|
| 勿 | 물 (mul) | 말 물 | Prohibition: "do not; must not" |

### 7.2 勿 Examples

| Sentence | Reading | Translation |
|----------|---------|-------------|
| 勿言 | 물언 | Do not speak |
| 勿忘 | 물망 | Do not forget |
| 勿食 | 물식 | Do not eat |
| 勿行 | 물행 | Do not go |

### 7.3 勿 in Classical Quotes

| Sentence | Reading | Translation | Source |
|----------|---------|-------------|--------|
| 非禮勿視 | 비례물시 | Do not look at what is improper | 論語 |
| 非禮勿聽 | 비례물청 | Do not listen to what is improper | 論語 |
| 非禮勿言 | 비례물언 | Do not speak what is improper | 論語 |
| 非禮勿動 | 비례물동 | Do not act on what is improper | 論語 |
| 己所不欲 勿施於人 | 기소불욕 물시어인 | What you do not desire, do not impose on others | 論語 |

### 7.4 Other Prohibition Words

| Word | 음독 | Meaning | Example | Translation |
|------|------|---------|---------|-------------|
| 勿 | 물 | Do not (standard) | 勿言 | Do not speak |
| 毋 | 무 | Do not (formal) | 毋忘 | Do not forget |
| 莫 | 막 | Do not (literary) | 莫言 | Do not speak |
| 不可 | 불가 | Must not; cannot | 不可行 | Must not do |

## 8. Double Negation Patterns

### 8.1 Double Negation = Emphasis

Two negatives make a strong positive in Classical Chinese:

> **Double Negation Patterns**
>
> - **不 + 不** = strong affirmation
> - **莫不** = everyone/everything (without exception)
> - **無不** = there is nothing that doesn't; all
> - **未嘗不** = has always (never not)
> - **不可不** = must (cannot not)
>
> - Example: 莫不知 = No one does not know = Everyone knows
> - Example: 不可不學 = Cannot not study = Must study

### 8.2 Common Double Negation Expressions

| Expression | Reading | Literal | Meaning |
|-----------|---------|---------|---------|
| 莫不知 | 막부지 | No one doesn't know | Everyone knows |
| 無不善 | 무불선 | Nothing is not good | Everything is good |
| 不可不學 | 불가불학 | Cannot not study | Must study |
| 不可不知 | 불가부지 | Cannot not know | Must know |
| 不亦樂乎 | 불역락호 | Is it not also joyful? | It is indeed joyful! |
| 不亦說乎 | 불역열호 | Is it not also pleasant? | It is indeed pleasant! |
| 無不為 | 무불위 | There is nothing not done | Everything is done |
| 未嘗不然 | 미상불연 | Has never not been so | Has always been so |

### 8.3 不亦...乎 Pattern (Rhetorical Double Negation)

This pattern creates a strong affirmation through a rhetorical question:

| Sentence | Reading | Literal | Meaning |
|----------|---------|---------|---------|
| 不亦善乎 | 불역선호 | Is it not also good? | It is indeed good! |
| 不亦美乎 | 불역미호 | Is it not also beautiful? | It is indeed beautiful! |
| 不亦樂乎 | 불역락호 | Is it not also joyful? | What joy! |

## 9. Negation in Classical Texts

### 9.1 From the Analects (論語)

```
子曰 知之者 不如好之者 好之者 不如樂之者
```

| Phrase | Reading | Translation |
|--------|---------|-------------|
| 知之者 | 지지자 | One who knows it |
| 不如好之者 | 불여호지자 | is not as good as one who loves it |
| 好之者 | 호지자 | One who loves it |
| 不如樂之者 | 불여락지자 | is not as good as one who delights in it |

> 不如 (불여) = "is not as good as; does not compare to"

### 9.2 From Mencius (孟子)

```
無惻隱之心 非人也
無羞惡之心 非人也
無辭讓之心 非人也
無是非之心 非人也
```

| Phrase | Reading | Translation |
|--------|---------|-------------|
| 無惻隱之心 | 무측은지심 | Without a heart of compassion |
| 非人也 | 비인야 | one is not [truly] human |
| 無羞惡之心 | 무수오지심 | Without a heart of shame |
| 非人也 | 비인야 | one is not [truly] human |
| 無辭讓之心 | 무사양지심 | Without a heart of deference |
| 非人也 | 비인야 | one is not [truly] human |
| 無是非之心 | 무시비지심 | Without a heart of right and wrong |
| 非人也 | 비인야 | one is not [truly] human |

> This passage uses both 無 (existence negation) and 非 (identity negation) together.

### Exercise 3: 不 vs 非 vs 無

Explain why each negation word was chosen:

```
1. 不知 vs 非知也 vs 無知
2. 不仁 vs 非仁也 vs 無仁
```

<details>
<summary>Click to reveal answers</summary>

```
1a. 不知 (부지) = does not know (negating the verb "know" — general fact)
1b. 非知也 (비지야) = it is not knowledge (negating identity — "this is not knowledge itself")
1c. 無知 (무지) = there is no knowledge; ignorance (negating existence of knowledge)

2a. 不仁 (불인) = is not benevolent (negating the quality — someone lacks benevolence)
2b. 非仁也 (비인야) = it is not benevolence (negating identity — "this act is not benevolence")
2c. 無仁 (무인) = there is no benevolence (negating existence — benevolence is absent)
```

</details>
