## 1. 所 — Relative Clause Marker

### Basic Function

所(소, so) transforms a verb into a noun phrase meaning "that which (is verbed)" or "what (is verbed)."

**Formula**: 所 + Verb = "that which is [verb]ed"

### Core Examples

| Expression | Reading | Literal | Natural Translation |
|-----------|---------|---------|---------------------|
| 所見 | 소견 (sogyeon) | that which is seen | what one sees, opinion |
| 所聞 | 소문 (somun) | that which is heard | what one hears, rumor |
| 所言 | 소언 (soeon) | that which is said | what is said |
| 所學 | 소학 (sohak) | that which is learned | what one has learned |
| 所爲 | 소위 (sowi) | that which is done | what is done, so-called |
| 所知 | 소지 (soji) | that which is known | what one knows |
| 所有 | 소유 (soyu) | that which is had | possessions, what one has |
| 所望 | 소망 (somang) | that which is hoped | hope, wish |

### 所 with Agents

When the agent performing the action needs to be specified, use the pattern:

**Formula**: A + 所 + Verb = "that which A [verb]s"

| Sentence | Reading | Translation |
|----------|---------|-------------|
| 吾所欲 | 오소욕 | what I desire |
| 人所不知 | 인소부지 | what people do not know |
| 天下所歸 | 천하소귀 | where all under heaven turn to |

### 所以 — "The reason why" / "The means by which"

所以(소이) is a crucial compound with two distinct meanings:

| Context | Meaning | Example |
|---------|---------|---------|
| Reason | the reason why | 所以然 (소이연) — the reason it is so |
| Means | by means of which | 所以治國 (소이치국) — the means by which to govern |

**Example from 맹자/孟子**:

```
人之所以異於禽獸者幾希
인지소이이어금수자기희
```

"That by which humans differ from beasts is but slight."

| Part | Reading | Function |
|------|---------|----------|
| 人 | 인 | Subject: humans |
| 之 | 지 | Possessive particle |
| 所以 | 소이 | that by which |
| 異 | 이 | differ |
| 於 | 어 | from (preposition) |
| 禽獸 | 금수 | beasts |
| 者 | 자 | nominalizer |
| 幾希 | 기희 | very slight |

## 2. 者 — Advanced Usages

### Beyond Simple Nominalization

者(자, ja) has multiple functions beyond its basic role of turning verbs or adjectives into nouns.

### Usage 1: Nominalizer — "one who / that which"

| Expression | Reading | Translation |
|-----------|---------|-------------|
| 仁者 | 인자 | one who is benevolent |
| 知者 | 지자 | one who is wise |
| 勇者 | 용자 | one who is brave |
| 學者 | 학자 | one who studies, scholar |
| 強者 | 강자 | one who is strong, the strong |
| 弱者 | 약자 | one who is weak, the weak |

### Usage 2: Topic Marker — Defines the subject of a judgment

**Pattern**: X者, Y也 = "As for X, it is Y" / "X is Y"

| Sentence | Reading | Translation |
|----------|---------|-------------|
| 仁者愛人 | 인자애인 | The benevolent person loves others |
| 學者不必爲師 | 학자불필위사 | A student need not become a teacher |
| 廉者不受嗟來之食 | 렴자불수차래지식 | An upright person does not accept food given with contempt |

### Usage 3: Pause/Enumeration Marker

When 者 appears after a clause, it creates a pause before the main predicate:

```
富與貴 是人之所欲也
부여귀 시인지소욕야
```
"Wealth and honor — these are what people desire."

### Usage 4: Temporal — "when / at the time of"

```
昔者聖王之治天下也
석자성왕지치천하야
```
"In former times, when the sage kings governed the world..."

## 3. 之 — The Versatile Particle

之(지, ji) is the most common function word in Classical Chinese, with at least four distinct uses.

### Usage 1: Possessive — "of, 's"

| Expression | Reading | Translation |
|-----------|---------|-------------|
| 天之道 | 천지도 | the Way of Heaven |
| 人之心 | 인지심 | the heart of a person |
| 國之本 | 국지본 | the foundation of the state |
| 學之道 | 학지도 | the way of learning |
| 仁義之人 | 인의지인 | a person of benevolence and righteousness |

### Usage 2: Pronoun — "it, him, them"

| Sentence | Reading | Translation |
|----------|---------|-------------|
| 知之 | 지지 | to know it |
| 取之 | 취지 | to take it |
| 愛之 | 애지 | to love him/her/it |
| 學而時習之 | 학이시습지 | to study and practice it regularly |

### Usage 3: Structural Particle — Connecting modifier to noun

When 之 connects a clause to a noun, it functions like a relative pronoun:

| Expression | Reading | Translation |
|-----------|---------|-------------|
| 所謂之 | 소위지 | what is called... |
| 可歎之事 | 가탄지사 | a lamentable matter |
| 不可不知之理 | 불가부지지리 | a principle that must be known |

### Usage 4: Rhythm Filler

Sometimes 之 is inserted purely for rhythmic balance, carrying no grammatical meaning:

```
頃之 (경지) — after a moment (之 is rhythmic)
久之 (구지) — after a long time (之 is rhythmic)
```

## 4. 以 — Instrumental and Causal

以(이, i) is a versatile preposition/conjunction meaning "by means of," "because of," "in order to," or "using."

### Core Meanings

| Function | Pattern | Example | Translation |
|----------|---------|---------|-------------|
| Instrument | 以 + N + V | 以劍殺之 (이검살지) | Kill him with a sword |
| Reason | 以 + clause | 以其無禮 (이기무례) | Because he was rude |
| Purpose | V + 以 + V | 學以致用 (학이치용) | Study in order to apply |
| Status | 以 + N | 以師禮待之 (이사례대지) | Treat him with the courtesy due a teacher |
| Standard | 以 + N + 爲 | 以仁爲本 (이인위본) | Take benevolence as the foundation |

### Key Compounds with 以

| Compound | Reading | Meaning |
|----------|---------|---------|
| 以爲 | 이위 | to consider as, to think that |
| 以來 | 이래 | since, from... onward |
| 以上 | 이상 | above, more than |
| 以下 | 이하 | below, less than |
| 以外 | 이외 | outside of, besides |
| 所以 | 소이 | the reason why; by means of |
| 可以 | 가이 | can, may |
| 足以 | 족이 | sufficient to |

### Example Sentences

| Sentence | Reading | Translation |
|----------|---------|-------------|
| 以德報怨 | 이덕보원 | Repay resentment with virtue |
| 不以物喜 不以己悲 | 불이물희 불이기비 | Do not rejoice over things, do not grieve over self |
| 何以知之 | 하이지지 | By what means do you know this? |

## 5. 於/于 — Preposition of Location and Comparison

於(어, eo) and 于(우, u) are interchangeable prepositions marking location, direction, comparison, or the passive agent.

### Functions

| Function | Pattern | Example | Translation |
|----------|---------|---------|-------------|
| Location | V + 於 + Place | 生於亂世 (생어난세) | Born in a chaotic age |
| Direction | V + 於 + Goal | 問於師 (문어사) | Ask the teacher |
| Comparison | A + 於 + B | 賢於人 (현어인) | More worthy than others |
| Source | V + 於 + Origin | 取之於民 (취지어민) | Take it from the people |
| Passive agent | V + 於 + Agent | 見笑於大方 (견소어대방) | Be laughed at by the wise |
| Time | 於 + Time | 於是 (어시) | At this point, thereupon |

### Comparison Pattern

The pattern X + 於 + Y means "X more than Y" or "X compared to Y":

| Sentence | Reading | Translation |
|----------|---------|-------------|
| 靑出於藍 | 청출어람 | Blue comes from indigo (the student surpasses the teacher) |
| 甚於水火 | 심어수화 | Worse than flood and fire |
| 人固有一死 或重於泰山 或輕於鴻毛 | 인고유일사 혹중어태산 혹경어홍모 | All must die; some deaths are heavier than Mount Tai, some lighter than a goose feather |

## 6. 而 — The Great Connector

而(이, i) is the most common conjunction in Classical Chinese, connecting clauses with various logical relationships.

### Functions

| Function | Meaning | Example | Translation |
|----------|---------|---------|-------------|
| Sequential | and, then | 學而知之 (학이지지) | Study and then know it |
| Adversative | but, yet | 學而不思 (학이불사) | Study but not think |
| Concessive | although | 貧而無諂 (빈이무첨) | Poor yet without flattery |
| Conditional | if | 人而無信 (인이무신) | If a person has no trust |
| Descriptive | and (manner) | 默而識之 (묵이지지) | Silently remember it |

### Distinguishing 而 Meanings

Context determines which meaning 而 carries. Key clues:

| Clue | Likely Meaning | Example |
|------|----------------|---------|
| Positive + positive | Sequential "and" | 仁而勇 (인이용) — benevolent and brave |
| Positive + negative | Adversative "but" | 學而不厭 (학이불염) — study but never tire |
| Negative + positive | Concessive "yet" | 不怒而威 (불노이위) — not angry yet imposing |
| Action + action | Sequential "then" | 起而行 (기이행) — rise and walk |

## 7. 則 — Conditional and Sequential

則(즉, jeuk) marks consequence, condition, or temporal sequence — "then," "thereupon," "in that case."

### Functions

| Function | Pattern | Example | Translation |
|----------|---------|---------|-------------|
| Conditional | A則B | 學則知 (학즉지) | If you study, then you know |
| Sequential | A則B | 天明則起 (천명즉기) | When dawn comes, then rise |
| Contrastive | A則B, C則D | 水則載舟 亦則覆舟 (수즉재주 역즉복주) | Water can carry a boat; it can also capsize it |
| Emphatic | 則 alone | 非我則誰 (비아즉수) | If not I, then who? |

### Important Patterns

| Pattern | Reading | Meaning |
|---------|---------|---------|
| A者B也 C者D也 | ...자...야 ...자...야 | As for A, it is B; as for C, it is D |
| 非...則... | 비...즉... | If not... then... |
| 不...則... | 불...즉... | If not... then... |

### Example from 논어/論語

```
君子不重則不威 學則不固
군자불중즉불위 학즉불고
```

"If the noble person is not dignified, then he will lack authority; if he studies, then [his learning] will not be superficial."

## 8. Combined Function Words

The true complexity of Classical Chinese emerges when multiple function words appear together.

### Common Combinations

| Combination | Reading | Meaning | Example |
|-------------|---------|---------|---------|
| 所以 | 소이 | the reason why / by means of | 所以然者 (소이연자) — the reason it is so |
| 所謂 | 소위 | what is called | 所謂仁 (소위인) — what is called benevolence |
| 之所以 | 지소이 | the reason that (someone/thing) | 人之所以爲人 (인지소이위인) |
| 以...爲 | 이...위 | to regard... as | 以德爲本 (이덕위본) — regard virtue as the root |
| 以...而 | 이...이 | by means of... and | 以禮而行 (이례이행) — act by means of propriety |
| 之於 | 지어 | (topic) regarding | 君之於民 (군지어민) — the ruler regarding the people |

### Complex Sentence Examples

**Example 1**:
```
所謂修身在正其心者
소위수신재정기심자
```

Parsing:
| Part | Reading | Function |
|------|---------|----------|
| 所謂 | 소위 | what is called |
| 修身 | 수신 | cultivating oneself |
| 在 | 재 | lies in |
| 正 | 정 | rectifying |
| 其 | 기 | one's |
| 心 | 심 | mind/heart |
| 者 | 자 | (nominalizer/topic) |

Translation: "What is meant by 'cultivating oneself lies in rectifying one's mind'..."

**Example 2**:
```
天下之所以治者 以有法也
천하지소이치자 이유법야
```

Parsing:
| Part | Function |
|------|----------|
| 天下 | Subject: all under heaven |
| 之 | Possessive |
| 所以 | the reason why |
| 治 | is governed |
| 者 | (nominalizer) |
| 以 | because |
| 有 | there exists |
| 法 | law |
| 也 | (assertion) |

Translation: "The reason all under heaven is governed is because there are laws."

## 9. Complex Sentence Parsing

### Parsing Strategy

When encountering a complex sentence:

1. **Find the main verb** — this is the structural center
2. **Identify function words** — they mark phrase boundaries
3. **Group into clauses** — 而, 則, 以 often separate clauses
4. **Determine relationships** — cause, condition, sequence, contrast
5. **Translate outward** — start from the main verb, then add modifiers

### Guided Parsing Practice

**Sentence**: 不以物喜 不以己悲 (from 범중엄/范仲淹: 岳陽樓記)

| Step | Action | Result |
|------|--------|--------|
| 1 | Find main verbs | 喜(희, rejoice), 悲(비, grieve) |
| 2 | Find function words | 不(불, not), 以(이, because of) |
| 3 | Parse clause 1 | 不 + 以 + 物 + 喜 = not because of things rejoice |
| 4 | Parse clause 2 | 不 + 以 + 己 + 悲 = not because of self grieve |
| 5 | Translate | "Do not rejoice because of external things; do not grieve because of personal [setbacks]" |

**Sentence**: 學而不思則罔 思而不學則殆

| Step | Action | Result |
|------|--------|--------|
| 1 | Main verbs | 罔(망, deluded), 殆(태, imperiled) |
| 2 | Function words | 而(이, but), 不(불, not), 則(즉, then) |
| 3 | Clause 1 | 學 而 不思 則 罔 |
| 4 | Structure | [Study] but [not think] → then → [deluded] |
| 5 | Clause 2 | 思 而 不學 則 殆 |
| 6 | Structure | [Think] but [not study] → then → [imperiled] |

## 10. Practice Exercises

### Exercise 1: 所 Constructions

Translate the following:

1. 吾之所見 (오지소견)
2. 人之所欲 (인지소욕)
3. 所以然者何 (소이연자하)
4. 學者所以明道 (학자소이명도)
5. 人之所以異於禽獸 (인지소이이어금수)

### Exercise 2: Identify 之 Functions

State whether 之 is possessive, pronoun, structural, or rhythmic:

1. 天之道 (천지도)
2. 取之 (취지)
3. 不可不知之理 (불가부지지리)
4. 久之 (구지)
5. 學而時習之 (학이시습지)

### Exercise 3: Parse Complex Sentences

Break each sentence into components and translate:

1. 以德報怨 (이덕보원)
2. 生於憂患 死於安樂 (생어우환 사어안락)
3. 學而不厭 誨人不倦 (학이불염 회인불권)

### Exercise 4: 而 Function Identification

Determine whether 而 means "and," "but," "although," or "if":

1. 學而知之 (학이지지)
2. 學而不思 (학이불사)
3. 貧而無諂 (빈이무첨)
4. 人而無信 不知其可 (인이무신 부지기가)

### Exercise 5: Full Sentence Translation

Translate the following passage, identifying all function words:

```
天時不如地利
천시불여지리
地利不如人和
지리불여인화
```

<summary>Answers</summary>

**Exercise 2**: 1) Possessive 2) Pronoun (it) 3) Structural (connecting clause to noun) 4) Rhythmic filler 5) Pronoun (it)

**Exercise 3**: 1) 以(with)+德(virtue)+報(repay)+怨(resentment) = Repay resentment with virtue 2) 生(live)+於(in)+憂患(worry) / 死(die)+於(in)+安樂(comfort) = One thrives in adversity and perishes in comfort 3) 學(study)+而(and)+不厭(not tire) / 誨(teach)+人(others)+不倦(not weary) = Study without tiring, teach others without wearying
