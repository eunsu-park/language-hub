## 1. Academic Vocabulary (학술 어휘)

Academic vocabulary is essential for reading Korean scholarly texts, university lectures, and TOPIK II reading passages.

### Core Academic Terms

| Korean | Hanja | English | Example Sentence |
|---|---|---|---|
| 연구 | 硏究 | research | 이 **연구**는 5년에 걸쳐 진행되었다. (This research was conducted over 5 years.) |
| 논문 | 論文 | thesis/paper | 석사 **논문** 주제를 정했어요. (I chose my master's thesis topic.) |
| 분석 | 分析 | analysis | 데이터 **분석** 결과를 발표했다. (They presented the data analysis results.) |
| 결과 | 結果 | result | 실험 **결과**가 예상과 달랐다. (The experiment results differed from expectations.) |
| 방법론 | 方法論 | methodology | **방법론**에 대한 논의가 필요하다. (Discussion on methodology is needed.) |
| 이론 | 理論 | theory | 새로운 **이론**을 제시했다. (A new theory was proposed.) |
| 가설 | 假說 | hypothesis | **가설**을 검증하기 위해 실험을 실시했다. (An experiment was conducted to verify the hypothesis.) |
| 실험 | 實驗 | experiment | **실험** 결과가 가설을 뒷받침했다. (The experiment results supported the hypothesis.) |
| 통계 | 統計 | statistics | **통계**에 따르면 인구가 감소하고 있다. (According to statistics, the population is declining.) |
| 조사 | 調査 | survey/investigation | 설문 **조사**를 실시했다. (A survey was conducted.) |

### Academic Verbs

| Korean | English | Usage |
|---|---|---|
| 분석하다 | to analyze | 자료를 **분석하다** |
| 조사하다 | to investigate/survey | 현상을 **조사하다** |
| 검증하다 | to verify/validate | 가설을 **검증하다** |
| 제시하다 | to present/propose | 의견을 **제시하다** |
| 입증하다 | to prove/demonstrate | 이론을 **입증하다** |
| 비교하다 | to compare | 두 그룹을 **비교하다** |
| 적용하다 | to apply | 이론을 **적용하다** |
| 평가하다 | to evaluate | 성과를 **평가하다** |
| 수행하다 | to carry out/perform | 연구를 **수행하다** |
| 파악하다 | to grasp/understand | 상황을 **파악하다** |

### Academic Sentence Patterns

| Pattern | Meaning | Example |
|---|---|---|
| -에 따르면 | According to... | 연구에 **따르면** 운동이 건강에 좋다. |
| -에 의하면 | According to... (formal) | 통계에 **의하면** 인구가 감소하고 있다. |
| -을/를 대상으로 | Targeting... / With... as subjects | 대학생 500명**을 대상으로** 조사했다. |
| -에 관한 / -에 대한 | Regarding / About | 환경**에 관한** 연구가 증가하고 있다. |
| -것으로 나타났다 | It turned out that... | 효과가 있**는 것으로 나타났다**. |
| -(으)ㄴ 바 있다 | Has (previously) done | 이 주제에 대해 연구**한 바 있다**. |

## 2. Politics and Government (정치·행정)

Political vocabulary is frequent in Korean news and TOPIK reading passages.

### Government Structure

| Korean | Hanja | English | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| 정부 | 政府 | government | 중앙 정부 (central government) |
| 대통령 | 大統領 | president | 한국은 **대통령** 중심제 (presidential system) |
| 국회 | 國會 | National Assembly | 한국의 국회는 단원제 (unicameral) |
| 국회의원 | 國會議員 | member of National Assembly | 300명의 **국회의원** |
| 장관 | 長官 | minister | 교육부 **장관** (Minister of Education) |
| 총리 | 總理 | prime minister | 국무**총리** |
| 시장 | 市長 | mayor | 서울특별시 **시장** |
| 도지사 | 道知事 | provincial governor | 경기**도지사** |

### Political Concepts

| Korean | Hanja | English | Example |
|---|---|---|---|

## 4. Environment (환경)

Environmental topics are among the most common themes in TOPIK II reading and writing sections.

### Core Environmental Terms

| Korean | Hanja | English | Example |
|---|---|---|---|
| 환경 | 環境 | environment | **환경** 보호가 시급하다. (Environmental protection is urgent.) |
| 오염 | 汚染 | pollution | 대기 **오염**이 심각하다. (Air pollution is serious.) |
| 기후변화 | 氣候變化 | climate change | **기후변화**에 대응해야 한다. (We must respond to climate change.) |
| 재활용 | 再活用 | recycling | **재활용**을 생활화해야 한다. (Recycling should become a way of life.) |
| 생태계 | 生態系 | ecosystem | **생태계**가 파괴되고 있다. (The ecosystem is being destroyed.) |
| 자연 | 自然 | nature | **자연**을 보호해야 한다. (We must protect nature.) |
| 온실가스 | 溫室— | greenhouse gas | **온실가스** 배출을 줄여야 한다. (We must reduce greenhouse gas emissions.) |

| Korean | English | Collocation |
|---|---|---|
| 보호하다 | to protect | 환경을 **보호하다** |
| 보존하다 | to conserve/preserve | 자연을 **보존하다** |
| 절약하다 | to save/conserve | 에너지를 **절약하다** |
| 감소시키다 | to reduce | 배출량을 **감소시키다** |
| 분리수거하다 | to sort waste for recycling | 쓰레기를 **분리수거하다** |
| 오염시키다 | to pollute | 강을 **오염시키다** |
| 파괴하다 | to destroy | 숲을 **파괴하다** |
| 복원하다 | to restore | 생태계를 **복원하다** |

### Types of Pollution

| Korean | English |
|---|---|
| 대기 오염 | air pollution |
| 수질 오염 | water pollution |
| 토양 오염 | soil pollution |
| 소음 공해 | noise pollution |
| 미세먼지 | fine dust (particulate matter) |
| 플라스틱 오염 | plastic pollution |

> **Cultural note**: 미세먼지 (fine dust/PM2.5) is a major public concern in Korea. Weather forecasts routinely include 미세먼지 levels, and many Koreans check the 미세먼지 forecast before going out. The vocabulary related to this issue (마스크를 착용하다 — to wear a mask, 공기 청정기 — air purifier) appears frequently in Korean media.

## 7. Word Formation Strategies

### 7.1 Using Hanja Morphemes to Decode New Words

By recognizing common Hanja prefixes and suffixes, you can decode unfamiliar compound words:

**Common Prefixes**:

| Prefix | Meaning | Examples |
|---|---|---|
| 불(不) | not, un- | **불**가능(impossible), **불**법(illegal), **불**편(inconvenient) |
| 무(無) | without, -less | **무**료(free), **무**인(unmanned), **무**한(unlimited) |
| 비(非) | non-, un- | **비**공식(unofficial), **비**정상(abnormal) |
| 재(再) | re-, again | **재**활용(recycling), **재**건(reconstruction), **재**시작(restart) |
| 초(超) | super-, ultra- | **초**고속(ultra-high speed), **초**대형(super-sized) |
| 최(最) | most, -est | **최**고(best), **최**대(maximum), **최**신(newest) |
| 신(新) | new | **신**제품(new product), **신**기술(new technology) |
| 구(舊) | old, former | **구**시대(old era), **구**제도(old system) |
| 반(反) | anti-, counter- | **반**대(opposition), **반**전(anti-war) |
| 다(多) | many, multi- | **다**양(diverse), **다**국적(multinational) |

**Common Suffixes**:

| Suffix | Meaning | Examples |
|---|---|---|
| -적(的) | -tic, -cal, -ive (adjective) | 과학**적**(scientific), 경제**적**(economic), 적극**적**(active) |
| -화(化) | -ization, -ification | 현대**화**(modernization), 세계**화**(globalization), 자동**화**(automation) |
| -성(性) | -ity, -ness (quality) | 가능**성**(possibility), 중요**성**(importance), 안전**성**(safety) |
| -학(學) | -ology, -ics (study of) | 과**학**(science), 경제**학**(economics), 심리**학**(psychology) |
| -가(家) | -ist, specialist | 전문**가**(expert), 작**가**(writer), 음악**가**(musician) |
| -자(者) | person who... | 소비**자**(consumer), 연구**자**(researcher), 기**자**(reporter) |
| -소(所) | place | 연구**소**(research institute), 사무**소**(office), 세탁**소**(laundromat) |
| -장(場) | place, field | 시**장**(market), 공**장**(factory), 운동**장**(sports field) |
| -비(費) | cost, expense | 교통**비**(transportation cost), 생활**비**(living expenses), 수업**비**(tuition) |
| -률/율(率) | rate, ratio | 성장**률**(growth rate), 실업**률**(unemployment rate), 출생**률**(birth rate) |

### 7.2 Decoding Unknown Words in Practice

**Example**: You encounter the word **비공식적** for the first time.

1. Break it down: **비**(非, non-) + **공식**(公式, official/formal) + **적**(的, -al)
2. Combine: non- + official + -al = **unofficial**
3. Verify with context: "비공식적인 만남" = an unofficial meeting ✅

**Example**: You encounter **재활용률**.

1. Break it down: **재**(再, re-) + **활용**(活用, utilization) + **률**(率, rate)
2. Combine: re- + utilization + rate = **recycling rate**
3. Verify: "재활용률이 높다" = the recycling rate is high ✅

## 8. Collocations: Common Verb-Noun Pairings

Korean collocations differ from English — you cannot always translate word by word. These pairings must be learned as units.

### Essential Collocations by Verb

**보다 (to see/take)**:

| Collocation | Meaning | NOT |
|---|---|---|
| 시험을 **보다** | to take an exam | ❌ 시험을 하다 |
| 꿈을 **보다** (rare) / 꿈을 **꾸다** | to have a dream | ❌ 꿈을 하다 |
| 신문을 **보다** | to read a newspaper | (읽다 also OK) |
| 손해를 **보다** | to suffer a loss | ❌ 손해를 받다 |

**내다 (to produce/put out)**:

| Collocation | Meaning |
|---|---|
| 결정을 **내리다** | to make a decision |
| 이익을 **내다** | to make a profit |
| 소리를 **내다** | to make a sound |
| 결과를 **내다** | to produce results |
| 용기를 **내다** | to muster courage |
| 돈을 **내다** | to pay (money) |

| Collocation | Meaning |
|---|---|

| 준비를 **하다** | to prepare |

### Exercise 2: Word Formation — Decode the Compound

Break each word into its Hanja components and determine the meaning.

1. 불가능 (不可能): 不 = ?, 可 = ?, 能 = ?
2. 재활용 (再活用): 再 = ?, 活 = ?, 用 = ?
3. 세계화 (世界化): 世 = ?, 界 = ?, 化 = ?
4. 최대한 (最大限): 最 = ?, 大 = ?, 限 = ?
5. 무료 (無料): 無 = ?, 料 = ?

<details>
<summary>Answers</summary>

1. 불가능: 不(not) + 可(possible) + 能(ability) = **impossible**
2. 재활용: 再(again) + 活(live/active) + 用(use) = **recycling** (re-use)
3. 세계화: 世(world) + 界(boundary) + 化(-ization) = **globalization**
4. 최대한: 最(most) + 大(big) + 限(limit) = **to the maximum extent**
5. 무료: 無(without) + 料(fee) = **free (of charge)**

</details>

## Key Takeaways

1. **Domain-specific vocabulary** is essential for TOPIK II — academic, political, scientific, environmental, medical, and economic terms appear repeatedly
2. **Hanja prefixes and suffixes** are the key to decoding unknown compound words: 불-(not), 무-(without), 재-(re-), -화(-ization), -적(-ical), -성(-ity)
3. **Collocations** must be learned as units — Korean verb-noun pairings often differ from English (시험을 보다, 스트레스를 받다, 이름을 짓다)
4. **Formal written Korean** uses Sino-Korean vocabulary heavily — recognizing these words is crucial for reading comprehension
5. **Word formation is systematic** — once you learn ~100 Hanja morphemes, you can decode thousands of compound words independently
