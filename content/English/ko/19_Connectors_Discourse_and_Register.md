## 1. 접속사 소개

### 1.1 접속사란?

접속사(연결어, 전환어, 담화 표지라고도 함)는 아이디어 간의 **논리적 관계**를 보여주는 단어와 구문입니다.

> 접속사 없이 (끊어지는 느낌):
> "I studied hard. I failed the exam. I was disappointed."
> 접속사 사용 (매끄러운 흐름):
> "Although I studied hard, I failed the exam.
> Consequently, I was very disappointed."
> 접속사는 응집성(COHESION)을 만듭니다 — 글과 말이
> 논리적이고 자연스럽게 흘러가게 합니다.

### 1.2 접속사의 유형

| 기능 | 예시 |
|------|------|
| 추가 | and, also, moreover, furthermore |
| 대조 | but, however, although, nevertheless |
| 원인 | because, since, due to, as |
| 결과 | so, therefore, consequently, as a result |
| 시간 | when, while, before, after, then |
| 조건 | if, unless, provided that |
| 목적 | to, in order to, so that |
| 예시 | for example, such as, for instance |
| 요약 | in conclusion, to sum up, overall |

### 1.3 문법적 분류

```
접속사 (절을 연결):
  등위 접속사: and, but, or, so, yet, for, nor (FANBOYS)
  종속 접속사: because, although, when, if, while, since

부사 / 부사구 (문장을 수식):
  However, Therefore, Moreover, In addition, Nevertheless,
  Consequently, Furthermore, On the other hand

전치사 (명사/동명사 앞에):
  Despite, In spite of, Due to, Because of, Instead of
```

## 2. 추가 접속사

### 2.1 유사한 아이디어 추가하기

> | 접속사 | 격식 수준 | 예문 |
> |---|---|---|
> | and | 중립 | "She sings and dances." |
> | also | 중립 | "He also speaks French." |
> | too | 비격식 | "I like it too." |
> | as well | 비격식 | "She's coming as well." |
> | in addition | 격식 | "In addition, the study |
> | found that..." |  |  |
> | moreover | 격식 | "Moreover, the data |
> | suggests..." |  |  |
> | furthermore | 격식 | "Furthermore, we must |
> | consider..." |  |  |
> | besides | 중립 | "Besides, it's too expensive." |
> | what's more | 비격식 | "What's more, she's smart." |
> | on top of that | 비격식 | "On top of that, he lied." |
> | not only...but also | 중립 | "Not only is she smart, |
> | but she's also kind." |  |  |
>
> ├──────────────────────┼───────────┼──────────────────────────────┤

### 2.2 부사적 접속사의 구두점

```
중요: "Moreover," "Furthermore," "In addition" 등은
접속사가 아니라 부사입니다. 쉼표만으로 두 절을 연결할 수 없습니다.

  ✗ "She is smart, moreover she is hardworking."
    (쉼표 접합 — 오류)

  ✓ "She is smart. Moreover, she is hardworking."
    (두 문장)

  ✓ "She is smart; moreover, she is hardworking."
    (세미콜론 + 부사)

  ✓ "She is smart, and moreover, she is hardworking."
    (접속사 + 부사)
```

## 3. 대조와 양보

### 3.1 대조 표현

> | 접속사 | 격식 수준 | 예문 |
> |---|---|---|
> | but | 중립 | "It's old but reliable." |
> | yet | 중립 | "She's young, yet wise." |
> | however | 격식 | "However, the results |
> | were inconclusive." |  |  |
> | nevertheless | 격식 | "Nevertheless, we must |
> | proceed." |  |  |
> | nonetheless | 격식 | "Nonetheless, the study |
> | has limitations." |  |  |
> | on the other hand | 중립 | "On the other hand, |
> | some disagree." |  |  |
> | in contrast | 격식 | "In contrast, Group B |
> | showed no improvement." |  |  |
> | on the contrary | 격식 | "On the contrary, the |
> | results were positive." |  |  |
> | while / whereas | 중립 | "While he prefers tea, |
> | she prefers coffee." |  |  |
> | instead | 중립 | "Let's walk instead." |
> | still | 중립 | "It was hard. Still, I |
> | managed." |  |  |
> | even so | 중립 | "It rained. Even so, we |
> | went out." |  |  |
>
> ├──────────────────────┼───────────┼──────────────────────────────┤

### 3.2 양보 (Despite / Although)

> 양보: 한 점을 인정하면서도 반대되는 것을 진술
> 접속사 (+ 절):
> although / even though / though + 주어 + 동사
> "Although it was raining, we went for a walk."
> "Even though she was tired, she kept working."
> "I enjoyed it, though it was long." (비격식, 끝 위치)
> 전치사 (+ 명사/동명사):
> despite / in spite of + 명사/-ing
> "Despite the rain, we went for a walk."
> "In spite of being tired, she kept working."
> 흔한 오류:
> ✗ "Despite she was tired..." (절이 따라올 수 없음)
> ✓ "Despite being tired..." (명사/동명사가 따라와야 함)
> ✓ "Despite the fact that she was tired..." (우회 표현)
> ✓ "Although she was tired..." (절에는 접속사 사용)

## 4. 원인과 이유

### 4.1 원인 표현

> | 접속사 | 격식 수준 | 예문 |
> |---|---|---|
> | because | 중립 | "I stayed home because it |
> | was raining." |  |  |
> | since | 중립/격식 | "Since you're here, let's |
> | start." |  |  |
> | as | 중립/격식 | "As it was late, we left." |
> | because of | 중립 | "Because of the traffic, |
> | I was late." |  |  |
> | due to | 격식 | "Due to the pandemic, |
> | classes moved online." |  |  |
> | owing to | 격식 | "Owing to the delay, we |
> | missed our flight." |  |  |
> | on account of | 격식 | "On account of his age, he |
> | retired." |  |  |
> | thanks to | 중립 | "Thanks to her help, I |
> | passed." |  |  |
> | for (접속사) | 격식/문어 | "We must act quickly, for |
> | time is running out." |  |  |
> | seeing that / | 비격식 | "Seeing that everyone is |
> | seeing as | here, let's begin." |  |
>
> ├──────────────────────┼───────────┼──────────────────────────────┤

### 4.2 "Because" vs "Because of"

```
because + 절 (주어 + 동사):
  "I was late because the traffic was heavy."
  "She cried because she was sad."

because of + 명사 / 명사구 / 동명사:
  "I was late because of the heavy traffic."
  "She cried because of the sad movie."

같은 구별이 적용되는 것:
  although / in spite of / despite
  while / during
```

## 6. 시간 접속사

### 6.1 사건 순서 나타내기

```
사건 이전:      before, prior to, previously, beforehand,
                until, up to that point

동시:           when, while, as, during, meanwhile,
                at the same time, simultaneously

사건 이후:      after, afterwards, subsequently, then, next,
                following that, since then

시작:           first, firstly, to begin with, initially,
                at first, in the beginning

중간:           then, next, after that, subsequently,
                following this, meanwhile

끝:             finally, lastly, eventually, in the end,
                at last, ultimately
```

### 6.2 예문

```
순차적:
  "First, preheat the oven. Then, mix the ingredients.
   Next, pour the batter into the pan. Finally, bake
   for 30 minutes."

동시:
  "While I was cooking, he was setting the table."
  "Meanwhile, the children were playing outside."

전/후:
  "Before leaving, make sure to lock the door."
  "After the meeting, we went for lunch."
  "I hadn't seen her since 2018."
```

## 8. 격식체 vs 비격식체

### 8.1 격식(Register)이란?

> **격식(Register) = 언어의 공식성 수준**
>
> 격식체:  학술 논문, 비즈니스 이메일, 공식 문서
> 중립체:  뉴스 기사, 일반 글쓰기, 직장
> 비격식체: 일상 대화, 문자, 소셜 미디어
> 잘못된 격식 수준을 선택하면:
> • 너무 딱딱함 (일상 대화에서 격식체)
> • 너무 가벼움 (비즈니스 이메일에서 비격식체)
> • 부적절함 (학술 논문에서 속어)

### 8.2 격식체 vs 비격식체 어휘

| 비격식 | 중립 | 격식 |
|--------|------|------|
| get | receive | obtain |
| buy | purchase | acquire |
| ask for | request | solicit |
| help | assist | facilitate |
| need | require | necessitate |
| start | begin | commence |
| end | finish | conclude/terminate |
| think about | consider | contemplate |
| go up | increase | escalate |
| go down | decrease | diminish |
| enough | sufficient | adequate |
| a lot of | many/much | numerous/substantial |
| about | approximately | circa |
| big | large | considerable/substantial |
| good | positive | favorable/beneficial |
| bad | negative | adverse/detrimental |
| show | demonstrate | illustrate/exemplify |
| use | employ | utilize |
| fix | repair | rectify |
| tell | inform | notify/apprise |

### 8.3 격식체 vs 비격식체 접속사

| 비격식 | 격식 |
|--------|------|
| and | moreover, furthermore, in addition |
| but | however, nevertheless, nonetheless |
| so | therefore, consequently, thus |
| because | due to the fact that, owing to |
| also | additionally, similarly |
| anyway | regardless, in any case |
| plus | additionally |
| like | such as, for example |
| basically | fundamentally, essentially |
| in the end | ultimately, in conclusion |

### 8.4 격식체 vs 비격식체 문장 구조

```
비격식체:
  "I can't come because I'm busy."
  "We need more time, so let's postpone."
  "There's a lot of stuff we haven't covered."

격식체:
  "I regret that I am unable to attend due to prior commitments."
  "Given the time constraints, it would be advisable to postpone."
  "Several topics remain to be addressed."
```

### 8.5 축약형

```
비격식 (축약형):              격식 (축약 안 함):
  don't, can't, won't          do not, cannot, will not
  I'm, she's, they're          I am, she is, they are
  it's, he'd, we've            it is, he would, we have
  gonna, wanna, gotta           going to, want to, have to
  (매우 비격식)

규칙: 격식 글쓰기(학술 논문, 사업 보고서, 공식 서신)에서는
      축약형을 피하세요.
```

## 9. 완화 표현 (Hedging)

### 9.1 완화 표현이란?

완화 표현은 절대적 주장을 피하기 위해 신중하고 조심스러운 언어를 사용하는 것입니다. 학술 및 전문적 의사소통에서 필수적입니다.

> 완화 표현 없이 (너무 강함):
> "Social media causes depression."
> 완화 표현 사용 (적절히 조심스러움):
> "Social media may contribute to depression."
> "Research suggests that social media could be linked to
> depression."
> "It appears that excessive social media use is associated
> with higher rates of depression."
> 한국어 학술 글쓰기는 더 직접적인 경향이 있습니다.
> 영어 학술 글쓰기에서는 완화 표현이 기대됩니다!

### 9.2 완화 표현 도구

```
조동사:
  may, might, could, would, can
  "This may indicate a trend."
  "The results could be explained by..."

부사:
  possibly, probably, perhaps, apparently, seemingly,
  arguably, presumably, generally, typically, often,
  sometimes, frequently, occasionally, partly
  "This is probably due to..."
  "The data perhaps suggests..."

완화 동사:
  seem, appear, tend, suggest, indicate, imply
  "The results seem to support the hypothesis."
  "The data suggests a correlation."
  "People tend to prefer familiar options."

완화 구문:
  it is possible that...     it appears that...
  it seems that...           it is likely that...
  it could be argued that... there is evidence to suggest...
  to some extent...          in most cases...
  on the whole...            to a certain degree...
```

## 10. 학술 담화 표지

### 10.1 학술 글 구조화하기

```
주제 소개:
  "This paper examines..."
  "The aim of this study is to..."
  "This essay will explore..."
  "The purpose of this research is..."

배경 진술:
  "It is widely acknowledged that..."
  "Previous research has shown that..."
  "It has been well documented that..."
  "According to Smith (2020),..."

논증 제시:
  "It can be argued that..."
  "One could contend that..."
  "The evidence suggests that..."
  "A compelling case can be made for..."

예시 제공:
  "For example, / For instance,"
  "...such as..."
  "This is illustrated by..."
  "A case in point is..."
  "To illustrate this point,..."

비교와 대조:
  "Similarly, / Likewise, / In the same way,"
  "In contrast, / Conversely, / On the other hand,"
  "Unlike X, Y..."
  "While X..., Y..."

인과관계 표현:
  "As a result, / Consequently, / Therefore,"
  "This leads to... / This results in..."
  "The consequence of this is..."
  "...thereby + 동명사"

요약과 결론:
  "In conclusion, / To conclude,"
  "In summary, / To summarize, / To sum up,"
  "Overall, / On the whole,"
  "Taking everything into consideration,"
  "The evidence presented suggests that..."
```

### 10.2 인용과 참고

```
출처 소개:
  "According to Kim (2023),..."
  "As Lee (2022) points out,..."
  "Smith (2021) argues that..."
  "Research by Park (2020) demonstrates that..."
  "As noted by Johnson (2019),..."

출처에 동의:
  "As Smith rightly argues,..."
  "This is consistent with findings by..."
  "Supporting this view, Lee (2022) found..."

출처에 반론:
  "However, this view has been challenged by..."
  "Contrary to Smith's assertion,..."
  "While Smith argues X, the evidence suggests Y."
```

## 11. 한국어 화자를 위한 참고사항

### 11.1 흔한 문제점

> 1. "and"와 "but"의 과다 사용
> 한국어 화자는 모든 것을 그리고/하지만으로 연결하는 경향
> 영어에는 훨씬 다양한 접속사가 있습니다!
> 2. 격식 수준 혼동
> 한국어에는 명확한 격식 수준이 있음 (존댓말/반말)
> 영어의 격식은 문법보다 단어 선택에 더 관련됨
> 3. 학술 글쓰기에서 완화 표현 누락
> 한국어 학술 스타일은 더 직접적일 수 있음
> 영어 학술 글쓰기에는 완화 표현이 필요
> 4. 쉼표 접합 오류
> ✗ "I was tired, however I kept working."
> ✓ "I was tired; however, I kept working."
> ✓ "I was tired. However, I kept working."
> 5. despite/although 혼동
> ✗ "Despite I was tired, I kept working."
> ✓ "Despite being tired, I kept working."
> ✓ "Although I was tired, I kept working."

### 11.2 한국어 접속사 대응 표현

```
그리고    → and / moreover / furthermore / in addition
하지만    → but / however / nevertheless
그래서    → so / therefore / consequently / as a result
왜냐하면  → because / since / due to the fact that
그러나    → however / yet / nonetheless
그런데    → but / by the way / however
또한     → also / moreover / additionally / furthermore
따라서    → therefore / thus / hence / accordingly
예를 들어 → for example / for instance / such as
결론적으로 → in conclusion / to conclude / to sum up
반면에    → on the other hand / in contrast / whereas
```

</details>

### 연습 2: 격식 변환

각 문장을 반대 격식 수준으로 다시 쓰세요.

1. 비격식: "I can't come 'cause I'm busy."
2. 격식: "We shall commence the proceedings at the designated time."
3. 비격식: "There's a ton of reasons why this won't work."
4. 격식: "It is imperative that all personnel comply with the regulations."

<details>
<summary>정답 보기</summary>

```
1. 격식: "I am unable to attend due to prior commitments."
2. 비격식: "We'll start on time."
3. 격식: "There are numerous reasons why this approach is unlikely
   to succeed."
4. 비격식: "Everyone needs to follow the rules."
```

</details>

### 연습 3: 오류 수정하기

1. Despite she was sick, she went to work.
2. I was tired, therefore I went to bed early.
3. Because of I forgot my wallet, I couldn't pay.
4. Although the rain, we enjoyed the trip.
5. He is smart, moreover he is hardworking.

<details>
<summary>정답 보기</summary>

```
1. Despite being sick, she went to work.
   또는: Although she was sick, she went to work.
   ("Despite"는 명사/동명사가 필요, 절은 불가)

2. I was tired; therefore, I went to bed early.
   또는: I was tired. Therefore, I went to bed early.
   ("Therefore"는 부사, 쉼표만으로 절을 연결할 수 없음)

3. Because I forgot my wallet, I couldn't pay.
   또는: Because of forgetting my wallet, I couldn't pay.
   ("Because of" + 명사/동명사; "Because" + 절)

4. Despite the rain, we enjoyed the trip.
   또는: Although it rained, we enjoyed the trip.
   ("Although" + 절; "Despite" + 명사)

5. He is smart; moreover, he is hardworking.
   또는: He is smart. Moreover, he is hardworking.
   ("Moreover"는 세미콜론이나 새 문장이 필요, 쉼표 접합 불가)
```

</details>

### 연습 4: 완화 표현 추가하기

다음 문장을 완화 표현을 사용하여 덜 단정적으로 만드세요.

1. "Social media is harmful to children."
2. "Exercise prevents heart disease."
3. "Students who study abroad are more successful."
4. "The results prove that the treatment works."

<details>
<summary>정답 보기</summary>

```
1. "Social media may be harmful to children."
   또는: "Research suggests that social media could be harmful
   to children."

2. "Regular exercise may help prevent heart disease."
   또는: "Exercise appears to reduce the risk of heart disease."

3. "Students who study abroad tend to be more successful."
   또는: "It has been suggested that studying abroad could
   contribute to greater success."

4. "The results suggest that the treatment may be effective."
   또는: "The results appear to indicate that the treatment
   could be effective."
```

</details>

### 연습 5: 단락 구성하기

적절한 접속사를 사용하여 다음 단락을 다시 쓰세요.

"The company's profits increased. The number of employees grew. Salaries were raised. The company expanded internationally. There were some challenges. Competition became fiercer. Costs increased. The company managed to maintain its growth."

<details>
<summary>정답 보기</summary>

```
The company's profits increased significantly. As a result,
the number of employees grew, and salaries were raised.
Furthermore, the company expanded internationally. However,
there were some challenges. For instance, competition became
fiercer and costs increased. Nevertheless, the company managed
to maintain its growth.
```

</details>
