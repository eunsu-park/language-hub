## 1. 관계절 소개

### 1.1 관계절이란?

관계절은 명사를 수식하는 종속절의 한 종류입니다. 명사에 대한 추가 정보를 제공하며, 관계대명사(who, which, that, whose, where, when)로 시작합니다.

```
┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│   주절:       "I met the woman."                                │
│   관계절:     "who lives next door"                              │
│   결합:       "I met the woman who lives next door."             │
│                                └──────────────────┘             │
│                                관계절                            │
│                                ("woman"을 수식)                   │
│                                                                 │
│   관계절에 의해 수식되는 명사를                                     │
│   선행사(ANTECEDENT)라고 합니다.                                   │
│   선행사: "the woman"                                            │
└─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘
```

### 1.2 관계절의 두 가지 유형

```
한정적 관계절 (Defining/Restrictive):
  어떤 명사를 말하는지 특정 — 필수 정보
  쉼표 없음
  "The book that I bought is interesting."
  (어떤 책? 내가 산 책.)

비한정적 관계절 (Non-Defining/Non-Restrictive):
  추가 정보 제공 — 특정을 위해 필수적이지 않음
  쉼표 사용
  "My sister, who lives in London, is visiting us."
  (언니는 한 명 — 관계절은 추가 정보)
```

## 2. 관계대명사

### 2.1 개요

| 대명사 | 용도 | 예문 |
|--------|------|------|
| **who** | 사람 (주격) | The man **who** called you is here. |
| **whom** | 사람 (목적격, 격식) | The person **whom** I met was kind. |
| **which** | 사물/동물 | The car **which** I bought is red. |
| **that** | 사람과 사물 | The book **that** I read was great. |
| **whose** | 소유 (사람/사물) | The girl **whose** bag was lost cried. |
| **where** | 장소 | The city **where** I was born is small. |
| **when** | 시간 | The day **when** we met was special. |
| **why** | 이유 | The reason **why** I called is important. |

### 2.2 Who vs That vs Which

```
사람의 경우:
  한정적 관계절에서는 "who" 또는 "that" 사용 가능
  비한정적 관계절에서는 "who"만 사용 ("that" 절대 불가)

  ✓ The student who passed the exam celebrated.     (한정적)
  ✓ The student that passed the exam celebrated.     (한정적)
  ✓ My brother, who is a doctor, lives in Seoul.     (비한정적)
  ✗ My brother, that is a doctor, lives in Seoul.    (오류)

사물의 경우:
  한정적 관계절에서는 "which" 또는 "that" 사용 가능
  비한정적 관계절에서는 "which"만 사용 ("that" 절대 불가)

  ✓ The movie which I watched was boring.            (한정적)
  ✓ The movie that I watched was boring.             (한정적)
  ✓ This book, which I bought yesterday, is great.   (비한정적)
  ✗ This book, that I bought yesterday, is great.    (오류)
```

## 3. 한정적 관계절

### 3.1 특성

```
┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│   한정적 관계절 (Defining Relative Clauses):                      │
│                                                                 │
│   • 쉼표 없음                                                    │
│   • 필수 정보 — 명사를 특정                                       │
│   • who, which, that, whose, where, when 사용 가능               │
│   • "that"가 "who"나 "which"를 대체 가능                          │
│   • 관계대명사가 목적어일 때 생략 가능                               │
│   • 절을 제거하면 의미가 바뀜                                      │
│                                                                 │
│   "The man who stole my car was arrested."                      │
│   → 어떤 남자? 내 차를 훔친 남자.                                  │
│   → 관계절 없이: "The man was arrested."                          │
│     (어떤 남자? 알 수 없음!)                                      │
└─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘
```

### 3.2 주격 관계절 vs 목적격 관계절

```
주격 관계절 (대명사 = 관계절의 주어):
  "The woman [who lives next door] is friendly."
   └─선행사──┘ └──주어──┘
   그 여자가 옆집에 산다 → 그녀는 친절하다

목적격 관계절 (대명사 = 관계절의 목적어):
  "The book [which I bought] is interesting."
   └선행사┘       └목적어┘
   내가 그 책을 샀다 → 그것은 재미있다
```

### 3.3 예문

```
주격:
  "The teacher who teaches math is strict."
  "The dog that bit me was brown."
  "Students who study hard get good grades."

목적격:
  "The movie (that) we watched was funny."
  "The cake (which) she made was delicious."
  "The man (who/whom) I met was very tall."

참고: 목적격 관계절에서 대명사는 일상 영어에서 자주 생략됩니다
      (위에서 괄호로 표시).
```

## 4. 비한정적 관계절

### 4.1 특성

```
┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│   비한정적 관계절 (Non-Defining Relative Clauses):                │
│                                                                 │
│   • 쉼표 사용 (절 앞뒤에)                                        │
│   • 추가 정보 — 특정을 위해 필수적이지 않음                         │
│   • who, which, whose, where, when 사용 가능                    │
│   • "that" 사용 불가                                             │
│   • 관계대명사 생략 불가                                           │
│   • 절을 제거해도 핵심 의미 변하지 않음                              │
│                                                                 │
│   "My mother, who is 60 years old, still works."                │
│   → 어머니는 한 분 — "who is 60"은 추가 정보                      │
│   → 절 없이: "My mother still works." (여전히 의미 통함)           │
└─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘
```

### 4.2 일반적인 용법

```
고유명사 뒤에:
  "Seoul, which is the capital of South Korea, has 10 million people."
  "Shakespeare, who was born in 1564, wrote 37 plays."

유일한 명사 뒤에 (my mother, the sun 등):
  "My car, which I bought last year, broke down."
  "The moon, which orbits the Earth, has no atmosphere."

추가 코멘트:
  "He passed the exam, which surprised everyone."
  "She quit her job, which was a brave decision."
```

### 4.3 절 전체를 가리키는 "Which"

비한정적 관계절에서 **which**는 앞선 문장 전체를 가리킬 수 있습니다:

```
"He arrived late, which annoyed the teacher."
 └────────────┘   └─which = 그가 늦게 도착한 사실─┘

"She speaks five languages, which is very impressive."
 └───────────────────────┘  └─which = 5개 언어를 하는 것─┘

"They cancelled the meeting, which meant I could go home early."
```

## 5. 관계대명사 생략

### 5.1 언제 생략할 수 있는가?

```
┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│   관계대명사를 생략할 수 있는 조건:                                 │
│                                                                 │
│   1. 관계절의 목적어일 때                                          │
│   2. 한정적 관계절일 때                                            │
│                                                                 │
│   목적어 (생략 가능):                                              │
│   "The book (that/which) I read was great."                      │
│   "The person (who/whom) she married is Korean."                 │
│                                                                 │
│   주어 (생략 불가):                                                │
│   ✗ "The man ___ lives here is nice."                            │
│   ✓ "The man who lives here is nice."                            │
│                                                                 │
│   비한정적 (생략 불가):                                            │
│   ✗ "My sister, ___ is a doctor, called me."                     │
│   ✓ "My sister, who is a doctor, called me."                     │
└─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘
```

### 5.2 주격과 목적격 구별법

```
방법: 관계대명사 뒤에 오는 것을 확인하세요.

  동사가 뒤따르면 → 주격 대명사 (생략 불가)
    "The woman who lives here..." (who + lives = 주격)

  주어가 뒤따르면 → 목적격 대명사 (생략 가능)
    "The woman (who) I met..." (who + I = 목적격)

추가 예:
  주격 (유지):  "The car that hit me was blue."
                                └동사가 뒤따름
  목적격 (생략): "The car (that) I hit was blue."
                                  └주어가 뒤따름
```

## 6. Whose, Where, When, Why

### 6.1 Whose (소유)

```
"whose"는 관계절에서 his/her/its/their를 대체합니다:

  "I know the man. His car was stolen."
  → "I know the man whose car was stolen."

  "The company whose products are the best won the award."
  "The student whose essay I read deserved an A."
  "This is the house whose roof was damaged by the storm."

참고: "whose"는 사람과 사물 모두에 사용 가능:
  사람: "The woman whose son won the prize was proud."
  사물: "The building whose windows were broken looked abandoned."
```

### 6.2 Where (장소)

```
"where" = in which / at which

  "This is the city where I was born."
  = "This is the city in which I was born." (격식)

  "The restaurant where we ate was excellent."
  "I remember the park where we used to play."

  비한정적:
  "Paris, where I spent my honeymoon, is beautiful."
```

### 6.3 When (시간)

```
"when" = in which / on which / at which

  "I'll never forget the day when I graduated."
  = "I'll never forget the day on which I graduated." (격식)

  "The year when she was born was 1995."
  "Do you remember the time when we got lost?"

  비한정적:
  "In 2020, when the pandemic started, everything changed."
```

### 6.4 Why (이유)

```
"why"는 "the reason" 뒤에 사용됩니다

  "The reason why I called is important."
  "That's the reason why she left."

  종종 축약됩니다:
  "That's the reason she left." ("why" 생략)
  "That's why she left." ("the reason" 생략)
```

## 7. 관계절에서의 전치사

### 7.1 비격식 vs 격식 스타일

```
비격식 (전치사가 끝에 — 대화에서 더 자연스러움):
  "The person who I was talking to is my boss."
  "The hotel which we stayed at was nice."
  "The topic that she was interested in was history."

격식 (전치사가 대명사 앞에 — 학술 글쓰기):
  "The person to whom I was talking is my boss."
  "The hotel at which we stayed was nice."
  "The topic in which she was interested was history."

격식 스타일 규칙:
  • 사람에는 "whom" 사용 ("who" 아님)
  • 사물에는 "which" 사용 ("that" 아님)
  • 전치사 뒤에 "that" 절대 불가
    ✗ "The hotel at that we stayed..."
    ✓ "The hotel at which we stayed..."
```

### 7.2 자주 쓰이는 동사 + 전치사 조합

```
talk to / about    "The person (who) I talked to..."
listen to          "The music (that) I listen to..."
look at            "The painting (that) she looked at..."
look for           "The keys (that) I was looking for..."
wait for           "The bus (that) we waited for..."
think about        "The problem (that) he was thinking about..."
work with          "The people (who) I work with..."
apply for          "The job (that) I applied for..."
depend on          "The friend (who) I depend on..."
belong to          "The club (that) I belong to..."
```

## 8. 관계절 축약

### 8.1 현재분사(-ing) 사용

능동 의미의 관계절을 -ing 구문으로 축약할 수 있습니다:

```
전체:   "The man who is standing by the door is my father."
축약:   "The man standing by the door is my father."

전체:   "Students who study abroad gain valuable experience."
축약:   "Students studying abroad gain valuable experience."

전체:   "The woman who was talking on the phone didn't notice."
축약:   "The woman talking on the phone didn't notice."
```

### 8.2 과거분사(-ed / 불규칙) 사용

수동 의미의 관계절을 과거분사 구문으로 축약할 수 있습니다:

```
전체:   "The book which was written by Tolkien is famous."
축약:   "The book written by Tolkien is famous."

전체:   "The houses that were built in the 1990s need repairs."
축약:   "The houses built in the 1990s need repairs."

전체:   "Food which is sold on the street is cheap."
축약:   "Food sold on the street is cheap."
```

### 8.3 To 부정사 사용

```
서수(first, second, last)와 최상급 뒤에:

전체:   "She was the first person who arrived."
축약:   "She was the first person to arrive."

전체:   "He was the last one who left the office."
축약:   "He was the last one to leave the office."

전체:   "She is the youngest person who has won this award."
축약:   "She is the youngest person to have won this award."
```

## 9. 명사절

### 9.1 명사절이란?

명사절은 문장에서 명사 역할을 하는 절입니다 (주어, 목적어, 보어).

```
주어로:
  "What she said surprised everyone."
  "Whether he will come is uncertain."
  "How they solved the problem remains a mystery."

목적어로:
  "I don't know where she lives."
  "She asked what time it was."
  "He believes that honesty is important."

보어로:
  "The question is whether we should go."
  "The truth is that nobody cares."
  "The problem is how we can afford it."
```

### 9.2 명사절 접속사

```
that:       "I know that she is right."
if/whether: "I wonder if/whether it will rain."
what:       "I don't understand what you mean."
who:        "Tell me who did this."
where:      "She asked where the station was."
when:       "I remember when we first met."
how:        "I know how to solve this."
why:        "Explain why you were late."
```

### 9.3 일반적인 패턴

```
"I think (that)..."        "I believe (that)..."
"I know (that)..."         "I hope (that)..."
"I'm sure (that)..."       "I'm afraid (that)..."
"I wonder if/whether..."   "I don't know if/whether..."

참고: 비격식 대화에서 "that"는 자주 생략됩니다:
  "I think she's right." = "I think that she's right."
```

## 10. 한국어 vs 영어 수식 구조

### 10.1 근본적인 차이

```
┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│   한국어: 전치수식 (수식어가 명사 앞에)                              │
│   영어: 후치수식 (관계절이 명사 뒤에)                                │
│                                                                 │
│   한국어:  [어제 내가 산] 책이 재미있다.                             │
│            [yesterday I bought] book is interesting              │
│            └──수식어──┘ └명사┘                                    │
│                                                                 │
│   영어: The book [that I bought yesterday] is interesting.       │
│         └명사┘  └───────수식어────────┘                           │
│                                                                 │
│   수식어와 명사의 위치가 정반대입니다!                                │
└─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘
```

### 10.2 실용 예문

```
한국어 어순 → 영어 어순:

  [서울에 사는] 친구        →  a friend who lives in Seoul
  [내가 좋아하는] 음악      →  the music (that) I like
  [빨간 지붕을 가진] 집     →  the house that has a red roof
                              the house with a red roof
  [작년에 지어진] 건물       →  the building (that was) built last year
  [영어를 가르치는] 선생님    →  the teacher who teaches English

  주의: 한국어는 긴 수식어도 모두 명사 앞에 놓습니다.
  영어는 긴 수식에 관계절을 사용하여 명사 뒤에 놓습니다.
```

### 10.3 한국어 화자의 흔한 오류

```
✗ "Yesterday I bought book is interesting."
  (한국어 어순을 영어에 적용)
✓ "The book (that) I bought yesterday is interesting."

✗ "In Seoul living friend is coming."
✓ "My friend who lives in Seoul is coming."

✗ "Red roof has house is beautiful."
✓ "The house that has a red roof is beautiful."

✗ "I know where is the station."
  (명사절에서 의문문 어순 사용)
✓ "I know where the station is."
  (명사절에서는 평서문 어순)
```

## 11. 연습 문제

### 연습 1: 한정적인가, 비한정적인가?

필요한 곳에 쉼표를 추가하세요. 쉼표가 필요 없으면 "쉼표 불필요"라고 쓰세요.

1. The woman who answered the phone was very rude.
2. My brother who is a pilot lives in Incheon.
3. The Eiffel Tower which is in Paris was built in 1889.
4. Students who cheat on exams will be expelled.
5. Seoul which is the capital of South Korea has a population of 10 million.
6. The car that I rented broke down on the highway.

<details>
<summary>정답 보기</summary>

```
1. 쉼표 불필요. (한정적 — 어떤 여자? 전화를 받은 여자.)
2. My brother, who is a pilot, lives in Incheon.
   (비한정적 — 형제가 한 명이라면 추가 정보.)
   또는: 형제가 여러 명이고 특정하는 경우 쉼표 불필요.
3. The Eiffel Tower, which is in Paris, was built in 1889.
   (비한정적 — 에펠탑은 하나뿐.)
4. 쉼표 불필요. (한정적 — 어떤 학생? 부정행위를 한 학생만.)
5. Seoul, which is the capital of South Korea, has a population
   of 10 million. (비한정적 — 서울은 하나뿐.)
6. 쉼표 불필요. (한정적 — 어떤 차? 내가 렌트한 차.)
```

</details>

### 연습 2: 관계대명사 채우기

who, which, that, whose, where, when 중에서 선택하세요.

1. The man ___ wallet was stolen reported it to the police.
2. I remember the day ___ we first met.
3. This is the restaurant ___ I had my first date.
4. The laptop ___ I use for work is very fast.
5. She is the teacher ___ inspired me to study English.
6. The country ___ flag has a dragon on it is Wales.

<details>
<summary>정답 보기</summary>

```
1. whose (소유 — "his wallet")
2. when (시간 — "on that day")
3. where (장소 — "at that restaurant")
4. that / which (사물 — 절의 목적어)
5. who / that (사람 — 절의 주어)
6. whose (소유 — "its flag")
```

</details>

### 연습 3: 문장 결합하기

관계절을 사용하여 결합하세요.

1. I bought a book. The book is about Korean history.
2. She lives in a city. The city has beautiful beaches.
3. I met a woman. Her husband works at Samsung.
4. That's the park. We used to play there as children.
5. He told me the reason. He quit his job for that reason.

<details>
<summary>정답 보기</summary>

```
1. I bought a book that/which is about Korean history.
2. She lives in a city that/which has beautiful beaches.
   (또는: She lives in a city where there are beautiful beaches.)
3. I met a woman whose husband works at Samsung.
4. That's the park where we used to play as children.
5. He told me the reason (why) he quit his job.
```

</details>

### 연습 4: 관계절 축약하기

가능한 곳에서 관계절을 축약하세요.

1. The woman who is wearing a red dress is my aunt.
2. The letter that was sent yesterday hasn't arrived.
3. People who live in glass houses shouldn't throw stones.
4. The car that is parked outside belongs to my neighbor.
5. He was the first person who climbed that mountain.
6. The problems that were caused by the storm were serious.

<details>
<summary>정답 보기</summary>

```
1. The woman wearing a red dress is my aunt.
2. The letter sent yesterday hasn't arrived.
3. People living in glass houses shouldn't throw stones.
4. The car parked outside belongs to my neighbor.
5. He was the first person to climb that mountain.
6. The problems caused by the storm were serious.
```

</details>

### 연습 5: 오류 수정하기

1. The man that car was stolen called the police.
2. My mother, that is a nurse, works at a hospital.
3. I know the place where did the accident happen.
4. The student which won the prize is from Korea.
5. She is the woman who I talked to she yesterday.
6. The reason because I was late is the traffic.

<details>
<summary>정답 보기</summary>

```
1. The man whose car was stolen called the police.
   ("that"가 아닌 소유의 "whose" 필요)
2. My mother, who is a nurse, works at a hospital.
   (비한정적 관계절에서 "that" 사용 불가)
3. I know the place where the accident happened.
   (명사절에서는 평서문 어순, 의문문 어순 아님)
4. The student who/that won the prize is from Korea.
   (사람에는 "who" 또는 "that" 사용, "which" 아님)
5. She is the woman who I talked to yesterday.
   (여분의 대명사 "she" 제거 — "who"가 이미 "the woman"을 가리킴)
6. The reason (why) I was late is the traffic.
   ("the reason" 뒤에는 "because" 아닌 "why" 사용)
```

</details>

### 연습 6: 한국어에서 영어로

관계절을 사용하여 한국어 문장을 영어로 번역하세요.

1. 서울에 사는 내 친구가 다음 주에 온다.
2. 내가 어제 읽은 책은 매우 재미있었다.
3. 창문이 깨진 집은 비어 있다.
4. 그녀가 일하는 회사는 매우 크다.

<details>
<summary>정답 보기</summary>

```
1. My friend who lives in Seoul is coming next week.
2. The book (that) I read yesterday was very interesting.
3. The house whose windows are broken is empty.
   (또는: The house with broken windows is empty.)
4. The company where she works is very large.
   (또는: The company (that) she works for is very large.)
```

</details>
