## 1. 조건문 소개

### 1.1 조건문이란?

조건문은 **조건**(if절)과 그 **결과**(주절) 간의 관계를 표현합니다.

> **조건문의 구조**
>
> IF절 (조건)              +    주절 (결과)
> "If it rains,"               "I will stay home."
> 순서를 바꿀 수 있습니다:
> 주절 + IF절 (쉼표 불필요)
> "I will stay home if it rains."
> 쉼표 규칙:
> If + 절, 주절.    ← if절 뒤에 쉼표
> 주절 if + 절.     ← 쉼표 없음

### 1.2 조건문 유형 총괄표

> | 유형 | If절 | 주절 | 의미 |
> |---|---|---|---|
> | 제로 | 현재 단순 | 현재 단순 | 항상 참인 사실 |
> | 제1 | 현재 단순 | will + 기본형 | 현실적/가능한 |
> | 제2 | 과거 단순 | would + 기본형 | 비현실 현재 |
> | 제3 | 과거 완료 | would have + PP | 비현실 과거 |
> | 혼합 | (조합) | (조합) | 혼합 시간 |
>
> ├────────┼──────────────────┼──────────────────┼─────────────────┤

## 2. 제로 조건문

### 2.1 형태

```
If + 현재 단순, 현재 단순

용도: 일반적 진리, 과학적 사실, 항상 일어나는 일
```

### 2.2 예문

```
과학적 사실:
  "If you heat water to 100°C, it boils."
  "If you mix red and blue, you get purple."

일반적 진리:
  "If you don't eat, you get hungry."
  "If people exercise regularly, they stay healthier."

습관적 결과:
  "If I drink too much coffee, I can't sleep."
  "If it rains, the streets flood." (항상 이렇게 됨)

지시/규칙:
  "If the light turns red, stop."
  "If the alarm goes off, leave the building immediately."
```

### 2.3 제로 조건문에서 "If" vs "When"

```
제로 조건문에서 "if"와 "when"은 종종 교환 가능합니다:

  "If/When you press this button, the door opens."
  "If/When I feel stressed, I go for a walk."

확실히 일어날 일에는 "when" 사용:
  "When water reaches 100°C, it boils." (확실한 일)

일어날 수도, 안 일어날 수도 있는 일에는 "if" 사용:
  "If the fire alarm rings, evacuate." (발생하지 않을 수도 있음)
```

## 3. 제1 조건문

### 3.1 형태

```
If + 현재 단순, will + 기본형 동사

용도: 현실적/가능한 미래 상황
      일어날 가능성이 있거나 가능한 일
```

### 3.2 예문

```
가능한 미래:
  "If it rains tomorrow, I will take an umbrella."
  "If you study hard, you will pass the exam."
  "If she calls, I will tell her the news."

경고:
  "If you touch that wire, you will get a shock."
  "If you don't hurry, you will miss the bus."

약속:
  "If you help me, I will buy you dinner."
  "If I get the job, I will celebrate."

협상:
  "If you lower the price, I will buy two."
```

### 3.3 주절의 변형

```
주절이 항상 "will"을 사용하는 것은 아닙니다:

  can:     "If you finish early, you can leave."
  may:     "If the weather is nice, we may go hiking."
  might:   "If she studies, she might get an A."
  should:  "If you feel sick, you should see a doctor."
  must:    "If you want to pass, you must study."
  명령문:  "If you see John, tell him I called."
```

### 3.4 중요한 규칙

> **if절에서 "will"을 절대 사용하지 마세요!**
>
> ✗ "If it will rain, I will stay home."
> ✓ "If it rains, I will stay home."
> ✗ "If you will study, you will pass."
> ✓ "If you study, you will pass."
> 예외: 의지/요청을 나타낼 때 if절에 "will" 가능:
> ✓ "If you will wait a moment, I'll check for you."
> (= 잠시 기다려 주실 의향이 있다면)

## 4. 제2 조건문

### 4.1 형태

```
If + 과거 단순, would + 기본형 동사

용도: 비현실적/가정적인 현재 또는 미래 상황
      일어날 가능성이 낮거나, 상상적이거나, 사실에 반하는 상황
```

### 4.2 예문

```
가정적 상황:
  "If I had more money, I would travel the world."
  (돈이 더 많지 않다 — 상상)

  "If I were you, I would accept the offer."
  (내가 당신이 아니다 — 불가능)

  "If I won the lottery, I would buy a house."
  (일어날 가능성이 낮음)

상상의 변화:
  "If I spoke Japanese, I would work in Tokyo."
  (일본어를 못 한다)

  "If she lived closer, we would see each other more often."
  (가까이 살지 않는다)

꿈:
  "If I could fly, I would visit every country."
  "If I had a time machine, I would go back to the 1990s."
```

### 4.3 제2 조건문에서 "Were" vs "Was"

> 격식적으로는 모든 주어에 "were" 사용 (가정법):
> "If I were rich, I would donate to charity."
> "If she were here, she would know what to do."
> "If he were taller, he could play basketball."
> 비격식 영어에서는 I/he/she/it에 "was"도 흔합니다:
> "If I was rich..." (비격식이지만 널리 수용됨)
> "If I were you" 구문에서는 항상 "were":
> ✓ "If I were you, I would study harder."
> ✗ "If I was you, I would study harder." (피하세요)

### 4.4 제1 조건문 vs 제2 조건문 대조

```
제1 조건문 (가능한/현실적):
  "If it rains tomorrow, I will bring an umbrella."
  (비가 올 수도 있다 — 현실적 가능성)

제2 조건문 (가능성 낮은/가정적):
  "If it rained in the Sahara, everyone would be shocked."
  (매우 가능성 낮음 — 가정)

제1:  "If I have time, I will help you." (시간이 있을 수도 있음)
제2:  "If I had time, I would help you." (시간이 없다)

제1:  "If she asks, I will tell her." (물어볼 수도 있음)
제2:  "If she asked, I would tell her." (아마 안 물어볼 것임)
```

## 5. 제3 조건문

### 5.1 형태

```
If + 과거 완료 (had + PP), would have + 과거분사

용도: 비현실적 과거 상황
      일어나지 않은 일에 대한 가정 (다른 과거를 상상)
```

### 5.2 예문

```
다른 과거를 상상:
  "If I had studied harder, I would have passed the exam."
  (열심히 공부하지 않았다 → 시험에 떨어졌다.)

  "If she had taken the earlier train, she wouldn't have been late."
  (일찍 기차를 타지 않았다 → 늦었다.)

  "If we had known about the problem, we would have fixed it."
  (몰랐다 → 고치지 못했다.)

후회:
  "If I had been more careful, I wouldn't have broken my leg."
  "If I had listened to my mother, I would have saved more money."

대안적 역사:
  "If Columbus hadn't discovered America, history would have been different."
  "If the internet hadn't been invented, life would be very different."
```

### 5.3 제3 조건문에서의 축약형

```
구어체 영어에서는 이 형태를 자주 축약합니다:

  "If I'd known, I'd have helped."
  = "If I had known, I would have helped."

  "If she'd called, I'd have answered."
  = "If she had called, I would have answered."

  참고: "I'd"는 "I had" (if절) 또는 "I would" (주절)를 의미
  문맥으로 의미를 판단합니다!
```

### 5.4 변형

```
could have / might have (would have 대신):

  "If I had practiced, I could have won."
  (= 이길 수 있었을 것이다)

  "If she had told me, I might have helped."
  (= 도와줬을 가능성이 있다)
```

## 6. 혼합 조건문

### 6.1 과거 조건 → 현재 결과

```
If + 과거 완료, would + 기본형 동사

의미: 과거에 일어나지 않은 일이 현재에 영향을 미침.

  "If I had studied medicine, I would be a doctor now."
  (의학을 공부하지 않았다 → 지금 의사가 아니다.)

  "If she had accepted the job, she would live in New York now."
  (수락하지 않았다 → 지금 거기 살지 않는다.)

  "If I hadn't missed the flight, I would be in Tokyo right now."
  (비행기를 놓쳤다 → 지금 도쿄에 없다.)
```

### 6.2 현재 조건 → 과거 결과

```
If + 과거 단순, would have + 과거분사

의미: 일반적/영구적 조건이 과거 사건에 영향을 미침.

  "If I spoke Chinese, I would have understood the conversation."
  (중국어를 못 한다 → 대화를 이해하지 못했다.)

  "If he were braver, he would have asked her out."
  (용감하지 않다 → 데이트 신청을 하지 않았다.)

  "If I weren't so shy, I would have talked to her."
  (내가 수줍음이 많다 → 말을 걸지 않았다.)
```

### 6.3 요약

> **혼합 조건문**
>
> 유형 1: 과거 → 현재
> If + had + PP, ... would + 기본형 동사
> "If I had saved money, I would be rich now."
> 유형 2: 현재 → 과거
> If + 과거 단순, ... would have + PP
> "If I were taller, I would have played basketball."

## 7. Wish와 If Only

### 7.1 Wish + 과거 단순 (현재에 대한 소원)

**현재**에 대한 소원을 표현할 때 사용합니다 (지금 달랐으면 하는 것).

```
"I wish I had more money."        (지금 돈이 충분하지 않다.)
"I wish I were taller."           (키가 크지 않다.)
"I wish I spoke French."          (프랑스어를 못 한다.)
"I wish I lived by the sea."      (바닷가에 살지 않는다.)
"I wish it weren't raining."      (비가 오고 있다.)

참고: 격식적으로는 모든 주어에 "were" 사용 ("was" 아님):
  "I wish I were..." / "I wish she were..." / "I wish it were..."
```

### 7.2 Wish + 과거 완료 (과거에 대한 소원/후회)

**과거**에 대한 소원을 표현할 때 사용합니다 (후회하는 것).

```
"I wish I had studied harder."        (열심히 공부하지 않았다 → 후회.)
"I wish I hadn't said that."          (그 말을 했다 → 후회.)
"I wish I had gone to the concert."   (가지 않았다 → 후회.)
"I wish we had met sooner."           (더 일찍 만나지 못했다 → 후회.)
```

### 7.3 Wish + Would (불만/변화에 대한 바람)

다른 사람의 행동에 대한 **짜증**이나 변화에 대한 바람을 표현할 때 사용합니다.

```
"I wish you would stop smoking."       (계속 피우고 있다 — 짜증남.)
"I wish it would stop raining."        (비가 그쳤으면 좋겠다.)
"I wish they would be quieter."        (시끄럽다.)
"I wish he would listen to me."        (귀를 기울이지 않는다.)

규칙:
  • 보통 다른 사람/사물에 대해 사용 (자신에 대해서는 아님)
  • ✗ "I wish I would be taller." (오류)
  • ✓ "I wish I were taller." (올바름)
  • 좌절감이나 변화에 대한 바람을 표현
```

### 7.4 If Only

"If only"는 "wish"의 더 강하고 감정적인 형태입니다.

```
현재 소원:
  "If only I had a car!"              = "I wish I had a car."
  "If only I knew the answer!"        = "I wish I knew the answer."

과거 후회:
  "If only I had listened to you!"    = "I wish I had listened."
  "If only we hadn't missed the train!" = "I wish we hadn't missed it."

불만:
  "If only he would call!"            = "I wish he would call."
  "If only it would stop snowing!"    = "I wish it would stop."
```

### 7.5 Wish 요약표

| 구조 | 시간 | 의미 | 예문 |
|------|------|------|------|
| wish + 과거 단순 | 현재 | 비현실 현재 | I wish I **spoke** English fluently. |
| wish + 과거 완료 | 과거 | 후회 | I wish I **had studied** more. |
| wish + would | 미래 | 불만/변화 바람 | I wish it **would** stop raining. |
| if only + 과거 단순 | 현재 | 강한 현재 소원 | If only I **were** rich! |
| if only + 과거 완료 | 과거 | 강한 후회 | If only I **had known**! |

## 8. 대체 조건 표현

### 8.1 Unless (= if not)

```
"unless" = "if ... not"

  "Unless you study, you will fail."
  = "If you don't study, you will fail."

  "I won't go unless you come with me."
  = "I won't go if you don't come with me."

  "Unless it rains, we'll have the picnic outside."
  = "If it doesn't rain, we'll have the picnic outside."

  ⚠ 부정 의미의 부정 주절과 함께 "unless" 사용 금지:
  ✗ "Unless you don't stop, I'll call the police."
  ✓ "Unless you stop, I'll call the police."
```

### 8.2 Provided that / Providing that

```
"provided (that)" = "only if" / "~라는 조건에서"
"if"보다 격식적

  "I'll lend you the money provided (that) you pay me back."
  "You can go out provided that you finish your homework first."
  "The event will proceed provided the weather is good."
```

### 8.3 As long as / So long as

```
"as long as" = "~하는 한" / "~라는 조건에서"

  "You can borrow my car as long as you drive carefully."
  "I don't mind waiting as long as it's not too long."
  "As long as you're happy, I'm happy."
```

### 8.4 On condition that / In case

```
"on condition that" = "if"의 격식 버전
  "I'll help you on condition that you help me in return."

"in case" = 예방 차원에서 / 일어날 수도 있으니
  "Take an umbrella in case it rains."
  (= 비가 올 수도 있으니까)
  ≠ "Take an umbrella if it rains."
  (= 비가 올 때만)
```

### 8.5 Even if / Whether or not

```
"even if" = 결과가 어차피 같음:
  "Even if it rains, we'll go hiking."
  "I wouldn't buy it even if I had the money."

"whether or not" = 조건에 관계없이:
  "Whether or not you agree, I'm going."
  "I'll call you whether or not I get the result."
```

### 8.6 Suppose / What if

```
"suppose" / "what if" = 이런 상황을 상상해 봐:
  "Suppose you won the lottery, what would you do?"
  "What if the plan fails?"
  "Suppose it rains, do we have a backup plan?"
```

## 9. 공손한 조건문

### 9.1 공손함을 위한 조건문 사용

```
제2 조건문으로 공손한 요청:
  직접적:    "Can you help me?"
  공손한:    "Could you help me?"
  매우 공손: "Would you mind helping me?"

  "It would be great if you could send me the report."
  "I would appreciate it if you would reply soon."
  "If you wouldn't mind, could you close the window?"

"I wonder if..." (매우 공손):
  "I wonder if you could help me."
  "I was wondering if you could spare a moment."
```

### 9.2 일반적인 공손 패턴

```
"Would you mind if I...?"
  "Would you mind if I opened the window?"
  "Would you mind if I sat here?"
  (참고: 과거 시제 "opened/sat"가 더 공손)

"If it's not too much trouble..."
  "If it's not too much trouble, could you send me a copy?"

"Would it be possible to...?"
  "Would it be possible to reschedule the meeting?"
```

## 10. 한국어 화자를 위한 참고사항

### 10.1 주요 차이점

> 한국어 조건문은 -(으)면을 사용합니다:
> 비가 오면, 집에 있을 거야.
> "If it rains, I will stay home."
> 한국어는 가정적 조건에서 시제를 바꾸지 않습니다:
> 내가 부자면, 여행을 갈 텐데.
> "If I were rich, I would travel." (한국어에는 과거 시제 없음)
> 이것이 한국어 화자들이 다음과 같은 오류를 범하는 이유입니다:
> ✗ "If I am rich, I would travel."
> (제1과 제2 조건문 혼용)
> ✓ "If I were rich, I would travel."
> ✗ "If I study harder, I would pass."
> (제1과 제2 조건문 혼용)
> ✓ "If I studied harder, I would pass." (가정)
> ✓ "If I study harder, I will pass." (현실적 가능성)

### 10.2 한국어 화자의 흔한 오류

```
오류 1: if절에서 "will" 사용
  ✗ "If it will rain, I will stay home."
  ✓ "If it rains, I will stay home."

오류 2: 가정적 상황에서 시제 후퇴 안 함
  ✗ "If I have more time, I would help you."
  ✓ "If I had more time, I would help you."

오류 3: 제3 조건문에서 "have" 빠뜨림
  ✗ "If I had studied, I would passed."
  ✓ "If I had studied, I would have passed."

오류 4: if절에서 "would" 사용
  ✗ "If I would know, I would tell you."
  ✓ "If I knew, I would tell you."

오류 5: "wish" 시제 혼동
  ✗ "I wish I can speak English better."
  ✓ "I wish I could speak English better."
```

## 11. 연습 문제

### 연습 1: 조건문 유형 식별하기

1. If you freeze water, it turns into ice.
2. If I had a million dollars, I would retire.
3. If she had come earlier, she would have met him.
4. If it rains, I'll take a taxi.
5. If I had taken that job, I would be living in London now.

<details>
<summary>정답 보기</summary>

```
1. 제로 조건문 (Zero Conditional) — 일반적 진리
2. 제2 조건문 (Second Conditional) — 비현실 현재
3. 제3 조건문 (Third Conditional) — 비현실 과거
4. 제1 조건문 (First Conditional) — 현실적 가능성
5. 혼합 조건문 (Mixed Conditional) — 과거 조건 → 현재 결과
```

</details>

### 연습 2: 조건문 완성하기

올바른 동사 형태를 채워 넣으세요.

1. If I ___ (be) you, I would apologize.
2. If she ___ (study) harder, she will pass.
3. If they ___ (not/miss) the bus, they would have arrived on time.
4. If you heat ice, it ___ (melt).
5. If I ___ (have) enough money, I would buy that car.
6. If he ___ (tell) me earlier, I would have helped him.
7. If it ___ (rain) tomorrow, we will cancel the picnic.
8. If I ___ (can) fly, I would visit every country.

<details>
<summary>정답 보기</summary>

```
1. were (제2 조건문 — "If I were you")
2. studies (제1 조건문)
3. hadn't missed (제3 조건문)
4. melts (제로 조건문)
5. had (제2 조건문)
6. had told (제3 조건문)
7. rains (제1 조건문 — if절에 "will" 불가)
8. could (제2 조건문)
```

</details>

### 연습 3: Wish 문장

"wish"를 사용하여 다시 쓰세요.

1. I don't speak Spanish. (하고 싶다.)
2. It's raining. (그쳤으면 좋겠다.)
3. I ate too much cake. (후회한다.)
4. My neighbor plays loud music. (짜증난다.)
5. I didn't go to university. (후회한다.)
6. I'm not taller. (키가 더 컸으면 좋겠다.)

<details>
<summary>정답 보기</summary>

```
1. I wish I spoke Spanish. (현재 소원 → 과거 단순)
2. I wish it weren't raining. / I wish it would stop raining.
3. I wish I hadn't eaten so much cake. (과거 후회 → 과거 완료)
4. I wish my neighbor would stop playing loud music. (불만 → would)
5. I wish I had gone to university. (과거 후회 → 과거 완료)
6. I wish I were taller. (현재 소원 → 과거 단순)
```

</details>

### 연습 4: 오류 수정하기

1. If I will have time, I will call you.
2. If I would know the answer, I would tell you.
3. If she studied harder, she will pass.
4. If I had knew about the meeting, I would have attended.
5. I wish I can play the piano.
6. If he hadn't been late, he would got the job.

<details>
<summary>정답 보기</summary>

```
1. If I have time, I will call you. (if절에 "will" 불가)
2. If I knew the answer, I would tell you. (if절에 "would" 불가)
3. If she studies harder, she will pass. (제1: 현재 + will)
   또는: If she studied harder, she would pass. (제2: 과거 + would)
4. If I had known about the meeting, I would have attended.
   (과거 완료 = had + 과거분사, "had knew" 아님)
5. I wish I could play the piano. (wish + 조동사의 과거형)
6. If he hadn't been late, he would have got/gotten the job.
   (would + have + 과거분사)
```

</details>

### 연습 5: "Unless"로 바꾸기

1. If you don't leave now, you'll be late.
2. If she doesn't apologize, I won't forgive her.
3. If it doesn't stop raining, the match will be cancelled.

<details>
<summary>정답 보기</summary>

```
1. Unless you leave now, you'll be late.
2. Unless she apologizes, I won't forgive her.
3. Unless it stops raining, the match will be cancelled.
```

</details>

### 연습 6: 혼합 조건문 연습

올바른 동사 형태로 완성하세요.

1. If I ___ (not/break) my leg last month, I ___ (play) in today's match.
2. If she ___ (be) more confident, she ___ (give) a better presentation yesterday.
3. If they ___ (invest) in Bitcoin in 2010, they ___ (be) millionaires now.

<details>
<summary>정답 보기</summary>

```
1. hadn't broken / would be playing
   (과거 조건 → 현재 결과)
2. were / would have given
   (현재 조건 → 과거 결과)
3. had invested / would be
   (과거 조건 → 현재 결과)
```

</details>

## 빠른 참조

> **조건문 빠른 가이드**
>
> Conditionals Quick Guide
> 제로:  If + 현재, 현재              (항상 참)
> 제1:   If + 현재, will + 기본형     (현실적/가능한)
> 제2:   If + 과거, would + 기본형    (비현실 현재)
> 제3:   If + 과거 완료, would have + PP (비현실 과거)
> 혼합:  제2와 제3의 조합
> WISH:
> 현재: wish + 과거 단순    "I wish I knew"
> 과거: wish + 과거 완료    "I wish I had known"
> 변화: wish + would       "I wish he would stop"
> 대체 표현:
> unless = if not
> provided that / as long as = ~라는 조건에서
> even if = ~에도 불구하고
> in case = 만일을 위해
> if절에서 "will"이나 "would"를 절대 사용하지 마세요!
