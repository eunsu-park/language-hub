## Prerequisites

- Knowledge of approximately 500+ kanji
- Familiarity with basic and intermediate vocabulary (Lessons 1-15)
- Understanding of kanji compound formation patterns (Lesson 18)
- Ability to read hiragana, katakana, and intermediate-level kanji

## Learning Objectives

- Build academic and research vocabulary (学術)
- Acquire society and politics terminology (社会)
- Learn science and technology vocabulary (科学技術)
- Expand health and medicine vocabulary (健康・医学)
- Develop media and communication terminology (メディア)
- Understand word formation patterns to independently expand vocabulary
- Apply thematic vocabulary in context through reading and writing

## 8. Word Formation Patterns

Understanding how Japanese creates new words from existing components helps you expand your vocabulary independently.

### 8.1 する Verbs (Noun + する)

Many nouns can become verbs by adding する:

| Noun | Meaning | Verb (する) | Example |
|------|---------|------------|---------|
| 研究 | research | 研究する | AIを研究する (to research AI) |
| 発表 | announcement | 発表する | 結果を発表する (to announce results) |
| 投票 | voting | 投票する | 選挙で投票する (to vote in an election) |
| 感染 | infection | 感染する | ウイルスに感染する (to be infected by a virus) |
| 検索 | search | 検索する | インターネットで検索する (to search on the internet) |

### 8.2 Compound Nouns (Noun + Noun)

| Pattern | Example | Reading | Meaning |
|---------|---------|---------|---------|
| Place + Event | 地震 | じしん | earthquake (ground + shake) |
| Object + Action | 読書 | どくしょ | reading (read + book) |
| Quality + Target | 高齢 | こうれい | advanced age (high + age) |
| Time + Object | 朝食 | ちょうしょく | breakfast (morning + food) |
| Scale + Thing | 大雨 | おおあめ | heavy rain (big + rain) |

### 8.3 -化 (かする) — "To ~ify / ~ization"

| Base Word | + 化 | Reading | Meaning |
|-----------|------|---------|---------|
| 国際 (international) | 国際化 | こくさいか | internationalization |
| 近代 (modern) | 近代化 | きんだいか | modernization |
| 自動 (automatic) | 自動化 | じどうか | automation |
| 高齢 (elderly) | 高齢化 | こうれいか | aging (of population) |
| 情報 (information) | 情報化 | じょうほうか | informatization |
| 温暖 (warm) | 温暖化 | おんだんか | warming |

### 8.4 Opposites by Prefix

| Pair | Reading | Meaning |
|------|---------|---------|
| 入院 ↔ 退院 | にゅういん ↔ たいいん | hospitalization ↔ discharge |
| 輸出 ↔ 輸入 | ゆしゅつ ↔ ゆにゅう | export ↔ import |
| 増加 ↔ 減少 | ぞうか ↔ げんしょう | increase ↔ decrease |
| 賛成 ↔ 反対 | さんせい ↔ はんたい | agreement ↔ opposition |
| 出発 ↔ 到着 | しゅっぱつ ↔ とうちゃく | departure ↔ arrival |
| 成功 ↔ 失敗 | せいこう ↔ しっぱい | success ↔ failure |

| カロリー | calorie | calorie |

> **Note:** Several Japanese medical terms come from **German**, not English (e.g., ウイルス,
> ワクチン, アレルギー, カルテ). This reflects the historical influence of German medicine
> on Japan's medical system during the Meiji era.
